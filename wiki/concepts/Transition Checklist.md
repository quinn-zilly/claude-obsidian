# Transition Checklist

**Transition checklist** — a structured tool that guides leaders through role transitions by specifying the actions most likely to produce rapid effectiveness and capture developmental lessons.

## Why Transitions?

Two reasons transitions are especially high-value for [[On-the-Job Development]]:
1. **Performance need** — people must perform effectively in new roles as quickly as possible
2. **Openness to learning** — people are maximally receptive to feedback and new information during transitions (Ashforth, Sluss, & Saks, 2007)

Transitions are therefore leverage points: same tool serves both performance acceleration and development.

## Checklist Contents (from Yost & Plunkett 2010)

Actions to take during transitions:
- Identify and meet with key stakeholders
- Get agreement on how success will be measured in the new job
- Seek feedback from others
- Identify new skills to be developed
- Build relationships with direct reports to understand their skills, performance, and development plans

## Delivery

Put online; integrate into:
- New employee orientation
- Leadership development programs
- High-potential programs
