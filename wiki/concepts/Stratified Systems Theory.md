# Stratified Systems Theory

**Stratified Systems Theory (SST)**, developed by Elliott Jaques, holds that organisations naturally stratify into levels of work distinguished by the **time-span of discretion** and the complexity of decisions/accountability at each level. [[bussin-swart-opperman-2022-job-evaluation|Bussin & Swart-Opperman (2022)]] describe SST as a "close cousin" of [[Paterson Job Evaluation|Paterson]]'s decision-band logic — both grade work by decision complexity and freedom to act rather than by task volume.
