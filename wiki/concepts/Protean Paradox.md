# Protean Paradox

The **Protean Paradox** (Hall, Yip & Doiron 2018) is the phenomenon whereby greater individual **self-direction and values orientation** — which serve the individual's own purposes and interests — can *also* have a **positive influence on the groups and organizations** in which the person works.

The paradox is that a career orientation centered on the person being in charge of their *own* destiny, rather than serving the organization, need not be zero-sum for the employer: identity-aware, adaptable, agentic employees can benefit the collective. Hall et al. flag it as meriting further investigation rather than treating it as established.
