# Work-Family Balance

A career/well-being parameter capturing the fit between work and family/nonwork roles — one of the three drivers (authenticity, **balance**, challenge) of the **[[Kaleidoscope Career Model]]** (Mainiero & Sullivan 2005).

The negative pole is **[[Work–Family Conflict]]** (bidirectional WIF/FIW). **[[kossek-su-wu-2017-opting-out-pushed-out|Kossek, Su & Wu (2017)]]** treat equal work–family balance as a nonwork outcome of **[[Women's Career Equality]]**, shaped by the **[[Ideal Worker Norm]]** and overwork culture. Note that **[[shockley-et-al-2017-gender-work-family-conflict|Shockley et al. (2017)]]** find men and women report near-equal WFC — see **[[Gender Similarity in Work–Family Conflict]]**.
