# Leadership Map

**Leadership Map** — a four-dimension taxonomy that expands standard competency models to include the full set of factors known to predict leadership effectiveness and development.

Introduced by [[Paul Yost]] & [[Mary Plunkett]] (2009, 2010). Response to limitation: most orgs have competency models but omit three equally important dimensions.

## Four Dimensions

| Dimension | Examples |
|-----------|---------|
| **Experiences** | Start-up, turnaround, corporate staff role, sales/marketing, global assignment |
| **Competencies** | Strategic thinking, building organizations, integrity, business acumen, judgment/decision-making, drive for results |
| **Key Relationships** | Senior executives, key customers, board of directors, government relations, union relations, community relations, mentor/champion |
| **Learning Capabilities** | Intellectual horsepower, openness to experience, integrity, comfort with ambiguity, openness to feedback |

## Purpose

- Self-assessment across all four dimensions (not just competency gaps)
- Guides succession planning
- Directs development planning
- Enables strategic thinking about one's own development trajectory

## Usage

Published org-wide so all leaders can use it. Not just HR tool — a shared language and navigation system for development.
