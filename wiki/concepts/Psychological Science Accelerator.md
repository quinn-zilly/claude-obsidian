# Psychological Science Accelerator (PSA)

Large-scale distributed collaborative network for psychological research. Advances psychology through massively multisite studies, dramatically increasing sample sizes and cross-cultural representation.

Founded by Moshontz et al. (2018). Published in *Advances in Methods and Practices in Psychological Science*.

Key function: provides infrastructure for replication and novel research at scale impossible for individual labs. Addresses the sample size and heterogeneity limitations that undermine replicability.

Related to [[Crowdsourcing Science]] (Uhlmann 2019) — both represent horizontal distribution of scientific labor.

Mentioned in [[nosek-2022-replicability-robustness-reproducibility]] as evidence of culture change infrastructure.
