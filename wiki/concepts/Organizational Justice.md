# Organizational Justice

## Justice vs. Fairness (Colquitt & Rodell 2015)

A definitional distinction increasingly drawn:

- **Justice** = perceived **adherence to rules** that reflect appropriateness in decision contexts (descriptive).
- **Fairness** = a **global perception of appropriateness** (evaluative), theoretically "downstream" from justice.

Historically the terms were used interchangeably. See [[Justice Measurement Taxonomy]] for how this distinction structures measurement.

## Why Justice Predicts Behavior: Two Lenses
