# APA Ethics Code

The **Ethical Principles of Psychologists and Code of Conduct** issued by the [[American Psychological Association]] — the governing ethics framework for U.S. psychologists (current version: 2002, amended 2010 & 2016, effective 2017). Full breakdown: [[apa-2017-ethical-principles-code-of-conduct]].

Three layers: an aspirational **Preamble + [[Five General Principles (APA)|five General Principles]]**, and **enforceable Ethical Standards** in ten sections (see [[Aspirational vs Enforceable Standards]]).

Its preamble commits psychology to improving "the condition of individuals, organizations, and society" — the normative anchor [[Joel Lefkowitz]] uses to critique I/O psychology's [[Scientist-Practitioner Model|narrower values]].
