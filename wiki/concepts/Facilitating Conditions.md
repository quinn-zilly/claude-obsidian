# Facilitating Conditions (FC)

The degree to which an individual believes that an organizational and technical infrastructure exists to support use of the system (Venkatesh et al. 2003). One of the four [[UTAUT]] constructs and a contextual factor (alongside social influence).

Concretely: help desks, training programs, technical support, infrastructure.

## Contested paths

- In original [[UTAUT]], FC predicts only **usage behaviour**, not intention — Venkatesh argued effort expectancy absorbs the FC → BI effect.
- [[dwivedi-2019-utaut-revision|Dwivedi et al. (2019)]] find FC predicts **behavioural intention** even with EE present, and add **FC → BI** to the [[Revised UTAUT Model]].
- They also find an emergent **FC → attitude** path: infrastructure like training and help desks shapes how users feel about the technology.
