# Glass Ceiling

The invisible barrier that keeps women (and other underrepresented groups) from advancing past a certain hierarchical level despite qualifications — i.e., **vertical segregation** in the leadership hierarchy.

In **[[kossek-su-wu-2017-opting-out-pushed-out|Kossek, Su & Wu (2017)]]** it is the failure of **vertical integration** (see **[[Vertical and Horizontal Gender Segregation]]**), reproduced by unequal access to power, networks, and opportunities (Kanter 1977). Related but distinct from the **[[Glass Cliff]]** (women appointed to precarious/failing leadership roles).
