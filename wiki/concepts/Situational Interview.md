# Situational Interview

Nav: [[index]] | [[Goal-Setting Theory]]

Selection method developed by Latham, Saari, Pursell & Campion (1980). Based on goal-setting theory: applicant intentions/goals predict behavior.

## Design

1. Conduct job analysis to identify critical situations
2. Present applicants with job-relevant dilemmas
3. Each question assesses the applicant's **goal or intention** for what they would do in that situation
4. Responses scored against behaviorally anchored rating scales

## Validity

- Meta-analyses: highest criterion-related validity of any interview type (McDaniel, Whetzel, Schmidt & Maurer 1994; Huffcutt & Arthur 1994; Latham & Sue-Chan 1999)
- Grounded in GST: conscious intentions are the most proximal determinant of action
