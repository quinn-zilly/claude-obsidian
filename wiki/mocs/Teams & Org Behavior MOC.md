# Teams & Organizational Behavior — Map of Content

> A Map of Content (MOC) links into a cluster of related notes. It is a navigation hub, not a container.

## Why this MOC exists

This cluster maps how groups in organizations adapt, coordinate, remember, and lead. Following these links answers: how do teams process information and adapt to disruption, what emergent states drive effectiveness, and how is leadership distributed and developed?
