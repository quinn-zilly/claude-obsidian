> A Map of Content (MOC) links into a cluster of related notes. It is a navigation hub, not a container.

## Why this MOC exists

This cluster maps how people learn, how training works (and transfers), and how development happens on the job. Following these links answers: what makes training effective, why most development is experiential, and which cognitive principles (spacing, testing, desirable difficulties) underpin durable learning.
