# Leadership — Map of Content

> A Map of Content (MOC) links into a cluster of related notes. It is a navigation hub, not a container.

## Why this MOC exists

This cluster maps how leadership is defined, distributed, co-produced, and studied. It pulls together the traditional leader-centric paradigms, the shift toward relational and shared conceptions, and the emerging study of followership — following the arc from "leadership is a property of the individual" to "leadership is co-constructed in leading–following interaction."
