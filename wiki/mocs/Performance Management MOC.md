# Performance Management — Map of Content

> A Map of Content (MOC) links into a cluster of related notes. It is a navigation hub, not a container.

## Why this MOC exists

This cluster maps how organizations evaluate and manage employee performance — and the century-long finding that better *measurement* has never been the answer. Following these links answers: why rating formats and rater-error training largely failed, why "performance management" has little proven impact on firm outcomes, where the real value lives (manager behavior and decisions), and how the field is now trying to define "effective PM" at all.
