# Multilevel Theory & Methods — Map of Content

> A Map of Content (MOC) links into a cluster of related notes. It is a navigation hub, not a container.

## Why this MOC exists

Organizations are **multilevel systems**: individuals nested in teams, teams in organizations, organizations in environments. This cluster maps the paradigm that bridges the **micro–macro gap** — how higher-level context shapes lower levels (top-down) and how lower-level action **emerges** into collective phenomena (bottom-up), and how to conceptualize, measure, sample, and analyze such models without committing levels fallacies.
