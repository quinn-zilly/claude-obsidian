# Edward L. Deci

Psychologist at University of Rochester; co-developer of [[Self-Determination Theory]] with [[Richard Ryan]].

Primary contributions: early reward experiments (1975 *Intrinsic Motivation*), [[Cognitive Evaluation Theory]], [[Organismic Integration Theory]], and the 1985 foundational book with Ryan. Demonstrated that tangible contingent rewards undermine intrinsic motivation — later confirmed by 1999 meta-analysis (Deci, Koestner & Ryan).

Key contributions in applied contexts: autonomy support in health care (medication adherence, weight loss, diabetes), education, and workplace motivation.
