# Stephen E. Humphrey

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work Design (Concept)]]

IO psychologist (Florida State University at the time of the 2006/2007 papers; later Penn State). Co-developed the [[Work Design Questionnaire]] with Morgeson and lead-authored the definitive work-design meta-analysis, which integrated motivational, social, and contextual work characteristics and tested the Job Characteristics Model mediation chain.

## Key Contributions

- **Morgeson & Humphrey (2006):** co-developed the WDQ — the comprehensive 18-category / 21-factor work design measure.
- **Humphrey, Nahrgang & Morgeson (2007):** meta-analytic summary and theoretical extension of the work design literature (259 studies).
- Work on team composition and team task design.
