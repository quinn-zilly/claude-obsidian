# Rong Su

Vocational-psychology and individual-differences scholar (Purdue University at the time of the 2017 study), known for meta-analytic work on **gender differences in interests** — the "men and things, women and people" finding (Su, Rounds & Armstrong 2009, d ≈ .93 on the things–people dimension) that anchors the [[Career Preference Perspective (Gender)|career-preference]] account of occupational segregation.
