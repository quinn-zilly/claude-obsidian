# Greg R. Oldham

Nav: [[index]] | [[Job Characteristics Model]]

IO psychologist (Tulane University; previously University of Illinois). With J. Richard Hackman, co-originator of **Job Characteristics Theory** and the **Job Diagnostic Survey** (Hackman & Oldham 1975, 1976, 1980) — the dominant approach to job design for decades. Continued contributions on creativity, the physical work context, and job crafting.

## Key Contributions

- **Job Characteristics Theory** and the **JDS** (with Hackman).
- Research on creativity and the physical/organizational work context.
- **Oldham & Fried (2016):** senior-author retrospective and future-directions review of job design.
