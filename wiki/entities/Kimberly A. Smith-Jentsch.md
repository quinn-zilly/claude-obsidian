# Kimberly A. Smith-Jentsch

Applied training and team researcher. Known for work on team training, CRM, assertiveness training for aviation/military contexts, and practical training implementation science.

## Affiliation
University of Central Florida (psychology / I-O)

## Key Contributions
- [[smith-jentsch-2020-smart-investments-training|Smith-Jentsch (2020)]] — practitioner roadmap for training ROI through benchmarking
- Co-authored seminal work on crew resource management (CRM) training; assertiveness training for junior aircrew
- FAA, Navy, NASA consulting experience

## Signature Ideas
- "The devil is in the details" — training effectiveness is implementation-dependent
- Active practice is non-negotiable for skill acquisition (not just observation)
- Intra-organizational benchmarking: continual monitoring with consistent metrics is essential
- Transfer environment change (appraisal, supervisor support, timing) can silently kill once-effective programs
