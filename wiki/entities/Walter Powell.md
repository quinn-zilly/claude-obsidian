# Walter Powell (Woody Powell)

American sociologist and organizational theorist. Professor at Stanford University (Graduate School of Education and (by courtesy) Sociology). Co-author with [[Paul DiMaggio]] of the foundational 1983 paper on [[Institutional Isomorphism]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the theory of institutional isomorphism and the concept of the [[Organizational Field]] (DiMaggio & Powell 1983)
- Later foundational work on network forms of organization (Powell 1990: "Neither Market nor Hierarchy")
- Research on knowledge networks in biotechnology and science-industry linkages
- Editor (with DiMaggio) of *The New Institutionalism in Organizational Analysis* (1991)

## Institutional Affiliation

Yale University (at time of 1983 paper) → MIT → Stanford University
