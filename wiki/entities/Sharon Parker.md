# Sharon Parker

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work Design (Concept)]]

Leading contemporary work design researcher. Lead author of [[parker-morgeson-johns-2017-100-years-work-design|Parker, Morgeson & Johns (2017)]], the JAP centennial review. Australian Research Council Future/Laureate Fellow; based at the University of Western Australia (and later Curtin University).

Central to several work design streams covered in this vault: role orientation and flexible role definitions (Parker, Wall & Jackson, 1997), role breadth self-efficacy (Parker, 1998), the elaborated model of work design (Parker, Wall & Cordery, 2001), proactive behavior (Parker et al., 2006; 2010), and the [[Learning and Development Perspective on Work Design|learning/development perspective]] (Parker, 2014, *Annual Review of Psychology*). Co-author of the influential text *Job and Work Design* (Parker & Wall, 1998). Advocates a **proactive, dynamic, multilevel** view of work design.
