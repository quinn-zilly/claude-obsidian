# Yogesh K. Dwivedi

Information systems researcher at Swansea University (Emerging Markets Research Centre, School of Management). Lead author of [[dwivedi-2019-utaut-revision|Dwivedi et al. (2019)]], the [[Revised UTAUT Model|revised UTAUT]] paper that reinstates [[Attitude as Mediator|attitude]] into technology-acceptance theory via [[MASEM]].

Frequent collaborator with Nripendra P. Rana on IS/IT adoption and e-government research.
