# Gerard Saucier

Nav: [[index]] | [[entities/_index]]

Personality psychologist, University of Oregon. A leading figure in the **lexical approach** to personality structure and cross-cultural / cross-language studies of trait taxonomies. His work identified the **Big Six** structure in inclusive lexical studies (Saucier 2009) and the cross-linguistic **Big Two** (agency/communion).
