# Mark L. Davison

Mark L. Davison is a quantitative/educational psychologist (Department of Educational Psychology, University of Minnesota), known for **profile analysis** methods. With [[Ernest C. Davenport Jr.]] he originated **criterion profile analysis** (Davison & Davenport 2002), the procedure later extended to meta-analytic data.

## Key work in vault
- [[davison-davenport-2002-criterion-related-patterns|Davison & Davenport (2002)]] — the primary [[Criterion Profile Analysis|CPA]] paper.
- [[wiernik-et-al-2021-meta-analytic-cpa|Wiernik et al. (2021)]] — co-author; extends his and Davenport's CPA procedure to meta-analytic data.
