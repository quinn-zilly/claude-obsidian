# Stephen W. Gilliland

Management scholar (Eller College of Management, University of Arizona). Best known for the **justice of selection systems** and **applicant reactions** — the "justice paradox" that the most valid selection tools are often seen as least fair.

## Contributions

- Co-author, [[cropanzano-bowen-gilliland-2007-management-organizational-justice|Cropanzano, Bowen & Gilliland (2007)]].
- Gilliland (1993, 1994) — selection-fairness model; the procedural-justice rules candidates apply to hiring.
