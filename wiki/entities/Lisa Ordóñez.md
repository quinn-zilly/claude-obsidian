# Lisa Ordóñez

Organizational psychologist. University of Arizona (Eller College of Management). Research on emotions, regret, equity, and decision making in organizational contexts.

## Research Areas

- **Regret and reference points**: Connolly, Ordóñez & Coughlan (1997); Ordóñez & Connolly (2000) — salary comparison and fairness
- **Emotion and decision making**: Kugler, Connolly & Ordóñez (2012) — fear vs. anger risk aversion reversal for chance vs. social risk sources
- **Goal-setting and escalation**: broader research program on goals and unethical behavior (not covered in Connolly 2012)

## Key Papers (cited in Connolly et al. 2012)

- Ordóñez, Connolly & Coughlan (2000) — MBA salary offer satisfaction; social comparison effects
- Connolly, Ordóñez & Coughlan (1997) — regret, reference points
- Kugler, Connolly & Ordóñez (2012) — fear/anger reversal for chance vs. social risk
