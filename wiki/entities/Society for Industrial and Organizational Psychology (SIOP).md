# Society for Industrial and Organizational Psychology (SIOP)

The professional body for industrial-organizational psychology (Division 14 of the APA). In September 1997 SIOP commissioned the **Job Analysis and Competency Modeling Task Force (JACMTF)** — jointly sponsored by its Professional Practice and Scientific Affairs Committees — to investigate and review the practice of competency modeling. The task force's 2-year effort produced [[Shippmann-et-al-2000-practice-of-competency-modeling|Shippmann et al. (2000)]] and the [[Level of Rigor Scale (JACMTF)]].
