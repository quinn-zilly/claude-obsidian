# Thomas W. H. Ng

Organizational/careers researcher (University of Georgia at time of the 2005 paper; later University of Hong Kong). Lead author of the [[ng-eby-sorensen-feldman-2005-career-success-meta-analysis|2005 *Personnel Psychology* meta-analysis]] of career-success predictors — a Best Paper award winner (AoM Careers Division, 2004).
