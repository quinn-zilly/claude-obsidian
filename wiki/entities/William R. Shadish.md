# William R. Shadish

Nav: [[index]] | [[log]]

Methodologist (1949–2016), leading figure in experimental and quasi-experimental design, meta-analysis, and program evaluation. First author of *Experimental and Quasi-Experimental Designs for Generalized Causal Inference* (2002) with [[Thomas D. Cook]] and Donald T. Campbell — the successor to Cook & Campbell (1979) and the canonical modern statement of the [[Validity Typology]].

## Contribution here

Carried the Campbell tradition forward, broadening [[Construct Validity (Causal Generalization)]] and [[External Validity]] to all study elements per Cronbach, and adding effect-size concerns to [[Statistical Conclusion Validity]].
