# Russell E. Johnson

Nav: [[index]]

I/O psychologist. Department of Management, Michigan State University. Research on self-regulation, regulatory focus, justice, implicit motivation, and ego depletion at work.

## Key Contributions

- Regulatory focus and work outcomes meta-analysis (Lanaj, Chang & Johnson 2012)
- Ego depletion in organizational contexts — procedural justice rules are depleting; interpersonal justice rules are not (Johnson, Lanaj & Barnes 2014)
- Implicit motivation measurement in organizational research (Uhlmann et al. 2012; Johnson & Saboe 2011)
- Core self-evaluations and job satisfaction (Ferris et al. 2013)
- Moving from cognition to behavior (Johnson, Chang & Lord 2006)
- Moral licensing and ethical leadership breakdown via depletion (Lin, Ma & Johnson 2016)

## Major Works

- Lanaj, Chang & Johnson (2012) — regulatory focus meta-analysis
- Johnson, Lanaj & Barnes (2014) — depletion and justice behaviors
- [[wiki/sources/kanfer-frese-johnson-2017-work-motivation|Kanfer, Frese & Johnson (2017)]] — century of progress review
