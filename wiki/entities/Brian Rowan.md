# Brian Rowan

American sociologist of education and organizations. Long affiliated with the University of Michigan; earlier work at Stanford (where the 1977 paper was written).

## Key Contributions

- Co-author with [[John W. Meyer]] of [[meyer-rowan-1977-institutionalized-organizations|"Formal Structure as Myth and Ceremony"]] (*AJS* 1977), a founding text of the new institutionalism.
- Subsequent research applying institutional and organizational theory to **schools and educational organizations** — a paradigm case of [[Decoupling]] between formal structure (curricula, credentials) and instructional practice.

## Institutional Affiliation

University of Michigan (School of Education); formerly Stanford University.
