# Claude Steele

Social psychologist, originator of **[[Stereotype Threat]]** theory (Steele 1997, "A threat in the air"). Coined **"wise schooling"** — schooling sensitive to diverse students' experiences that defuses stereotype threat — the direct precursor to [[Gregory Walton]]'s generalized notion of [[Wise Interventions]]. The *wise* terminology itself traces through Steele back to Goffman (1963). Cited in [[Walton-2014-Wise-Psychological-Interventions|Walton (2014)]].
