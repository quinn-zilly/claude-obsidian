# Jennifer D. Nahrgang

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work Design (Concept)]]

IO psychologist (Michigan State at the time of the 2007 paper; later Arizona State / University of Iowa). Co-author of the 2007 work-design meta-analysis and of the Nahrgang, Morgeson & Hofmann (2011) JD-R safety meta-analysis.

## Key Contributions

- **Humphrey, Nahrgang & Morgeson (2007):** work-design meta-analysis and extension.
- **Nahrgang, Morgeson & Hofmann (2011):** JD-R meta-analysis linking demands/resources to burnout, engagement, and safety outcomes.
