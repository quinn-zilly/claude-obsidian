# Robin Hogarth

Decision-making researcher (ICREA / Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona). Major figure in [[Judgment and Decision Making (Field)|JDM]], known for work on the [[Lens Model]], heuristics vs linear models, and learning environments ("kind" vs "wicked"). With [[Natalia Karelaia]] authored the 2007 *Psychological Review* matching-rules paper and the [[karelaia-hogarth-2008-lens-model-meta-analysis|2008 lens-model meta-analysis]].
