# Colette A. Frayne

Nav: [[index]]

Industrial–organizational psychologist. PhD University of Washington (under [[Gary Latham]]). Subsequently faculty at University of Western Ontario.

## Key Contributions

- **Frayne & Latham (1987)** — first rigorous field experiment applying self-management training to employee attendance in an organizational (non-clinical) setting. Doctoral dissertation.
- Demonstrated [[Self-Management Training]] produces lasting gains in self-efficacy and attendance at 3-month follow-up.

## Major Works

- [[frayne-latham-1987-self-management-attendance|Frayne & Latham (1987)]] — *Journal of Applied Psychology*, 72(3), 387–392
- Latham & Frayne (1986) — stability of job attendance (AOM conference)
