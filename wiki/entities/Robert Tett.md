# Robert Tett

Department of Psychology, University of Tulsa (Tulsa, Oklahoma). I/O psychologist. Primary architect of [[Trait Activation Theory]].

## Key Contributions
- **Tett & Guterman (2000)**: original empirical test of TAT; cross-situational consistency as function of situation trait relevance
- **Tett & Burnett (2003)**: full conceptual model; first five functional cue types
- **Tett et al. (1991, 1999)**: meta-analyses on personality–job performance; confirmatory > exploratory validity; bidirectionality evidence
- **Tett & Christiansen (2007)**: bidirectionality in meta-analyses of personality
- **Tett et al. (2013)**: Handbook of Personality at Work chapter; 6th cue type (discretionary); TAT applications review through 2011
- **Tett & Fisher (2020)**: personality dynamics framework; five research lines × five TAT issues
- **Tett et al. (2021)**: Annual Review — [[tett-et-al-2021-trait-activation-theory]]
