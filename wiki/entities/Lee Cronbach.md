# Lee Cronbach

Psychometrician and evaluation theorist (Lee J. Cronbach). Named in [[Sechrest-Figueredo-1993-Program-Evaluation|Sechrest & Figueredo (1993)]] among the **early leaders of program evaluation** who intended the enterprise to use the most rigorous methods of social science (alongside [[Donald T. Campbell]], Peter Rossi, Richard Light, Carol Weiss). Best known in the vault's broader scope for foundational contributions to reliability and construct validity (cf. the reliability/validity cluster). A leading voice for external validity and generalizability in evaluation.
