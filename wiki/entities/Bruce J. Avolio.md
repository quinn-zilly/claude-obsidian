# Bruce J. Avolio

Organizational psychologist; prominent researcher on transformational and authentic leadership, leadership development.

## Key Contributions
- **Howell & Avolio (1993)** [JAP, Table 1]: Transformational leadership → business unit performance; locus of control as antecedent
- **Bass, Avolio, Jung & Berson (2003)** [JAP, Table 1]: Transformational → team potency and cohesion → team performance
- **Avolio et al. (2009)**: Meta-analysis across 75 years on leadership development interventions — all major styles are trainable
- Co-author of [[lord-et-al-2017-leadership-in-applied-psychology]] (JAP 2017 centennial review)

## Research Focus
Transformational and authentic leadership; leadership development; multilevel leadership; charisma and vision.
