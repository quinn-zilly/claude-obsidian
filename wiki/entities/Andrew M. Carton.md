# Andrew M. Carton

Nav: [[index]] | [[Leadership MOC]]

Management scholar, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. Studies leadership, vision, meaning, and meaningfulness at work. Author of the [[carton-2022-science-of-leadership|Annual Review meta-theoretical map of leadership]] (2022) built on the [[Leaders vs Leading|leaders-vs-leading]] and [[Leadership as Cause vs Consequence|cause-vs-consequence]] distinctions, and advocate of [[Lumped vs Split Conceptualizations of Leading|split over lumped]] conceptualizations.
