# Bryan J. Dik

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work as a Calling MOC]]

Counseling / vocational psychologist (Colorado State University at the time of the 2009 paper). With Ryan Duffy, co-authored one of the two founding articles of the modern calling literature, proposing measurable, multidimensional definitions of **calling** and **[[Vocation (Dik & Duffy)|vocation]]** for counseling psychology.

## Key contributions

- **[[Dik-Duffy-2009-Calling-and-Vocation|Dik & Duffy (2009)]]** — the "transcendent summons" definition of calling; the calling/vocation distinction; the [[Presence vs Search for Calling|presence vs search]] distinction.
- Co-developer of standard calling instruments (Brief Calling Scale with Steger; Calling and Vocation Questionnaire, Dik et al. 2012).
