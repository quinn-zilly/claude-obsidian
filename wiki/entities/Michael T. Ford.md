# Michael T. Ford

Organizational psychologist studying employee well-being, stress, work–nonwork interface, and the employee–organization relationship.

**Affiliation (as of 2024):** Department of Management, The University of Alabama.

## Key contributions

- **[[hong-ford-jong-2024-employee-benefits-meta-analysis|Hong, Ford & Jong (2024, *Journal of Applied Psychology*)]]** — played the lead role in conceptualization and supervision of the employee-benefits meta-analysis grounded in [[Social Exchange Theory]].

## Focus

Employee well-being and stress, [[Perceived Organizational Support|POS]], work–family interface, meta-analytic methods.
