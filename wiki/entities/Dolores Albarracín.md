# Dolores Albarracín

Social psychologist at the University of Pennsylvania (Departments of Psychology, Annenberg School of Communication, Annenberg Public Policy Center, School of Nursing, Wharton School). Research focuses on attitude change, persuasion, and behaviour change interventions, with extensive work on HIV prevention.

## Key Contributions

- Co-editor of *The Handbook of Attitudes* (with Johnson & Zanna)
- Meta-analytic work on HIV prevention interventions (2005 *Psychological Bulletin*)
- 2024 review synthesizing meta-analyses of behaviour change determinants across all domains — see [[albarracin-2024-determinants-behaviour-change]]
- Central finding: knowledge and attitude targets are overused; access and habit targets are underused
