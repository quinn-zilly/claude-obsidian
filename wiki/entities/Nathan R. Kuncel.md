# Nathan R. Kuncel

I/O psychologist (University of Minnesota). Works on selection, admissions, cognitive ability, and the validity of prediction methods. Lead author of the [[kuncel-et-al-2013-mechanical-vs-clinical|2013 mechanical-vs-clinical meta-analysis]], which reframes personnel selection through the [[Lens Model]].
