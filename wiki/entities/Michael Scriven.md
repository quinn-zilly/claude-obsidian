# Michael Scriven

Evaluation theorist and philosopher. Originated the **[[Formative vs Summative Evaluation|formative vs. summative]]** distinction (Scriven 1967), one of the most durable conceptual tools in program evaluation and a central topic in [[Sechrest-Figueredo-1993-Program-Evaluation|Sechrest & Figueredo (1993)]]'s discussion of evaluation practice.
