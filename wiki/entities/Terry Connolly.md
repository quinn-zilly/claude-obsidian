# Terry Connolly

Organizational psychologist. University of Arizona (Eller College of Management). Primary contributor to JDM research in organizational contexts.

## Research Areas

- **Regret and decision making**: co-developed [[Decision Justification Theory]] (with Zeelenberg 2002); action effect studies; regret priming effects
- **Electronic brainstorming**: early research on EBS; anonymity effects; group size threshold; [[Electronic Brainstorming]]
- **Naturalistic decision making**: Orasanu & Connolly (1993) — key NDM overview paper; see [[Naturalistic Decision Making]]
- **Information purchase**: Bayesian and regression models; underpurchase/overpurchase of decision-relevant information
- **Dynamic decision making**: multiple interventions over time; simulated environments

## Key Papers (cited in Connolly et al. 2012)

- Connolly & Zeelenberg (2002) — Decision Justification Theory
- Connolly, Ordóñez & Coughlan (1997) — regret and reference points
- Connolly, Jessup & Valacich (1990) — EBS anonymity effects
- Connolly (1997) — EBS group size
- Orasanu & Connolly (1993) — NDM overview
- Connolly & Miklausich (1978) — MCPL feedback error
- Connolly, Reb & Kausel (2010) — reason-based choice + regret salience
