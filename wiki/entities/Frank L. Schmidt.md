# Frank L. Schmidt

I/O psychologist (University of Iowa). With John Hunter, co-developed **Hunter–Schmidt [[psychometric meta-analysis]]** — the validity-generalization framework that corrects effect sizes for sampling error and measurement artifacts. Underpins both [[viswesvaran-schmidt-ones-2005-general-factor-job-performance|Viswesvaran et al. (2005)]] and [[kuncel-et-al-2013-mechanical-vs-clinical|Kuncel et al. (2013)]]. Not the same person as [[Aaron Schmidt]].

The Schmidt & Hunter (1998) summary of the [[Operational Validity (Selection)|operational validity]] of 19 selection procedures was the field's canonical benchmark for two decades. [[Sackett-et-al-2022-validity-overcorrection|Sackett et al. (2022)]] revisit and revise those estimates downward after correcting for systematic [[Range Restriction Correction|range-restriction]] overcorrection.
