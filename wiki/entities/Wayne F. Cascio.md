# Wayne F. Cascio

Industrial-organizational psychologist, known for work on the utility/economic value of HR interventions, criterion measurement, and applied psychology in human resource management. Co-author with [[Herman Aguinis]] of the widely used textbook *Applied Psychology in Human Resource Management*.
