# Yitzhak Fried

Nav: [[index]] | [[Job Characteristics Model]]

Management/IO scholar (Texas Tech University, Rawls College of Business). Co-author of the influential **Fried & Ferris (1987)** meta-analysis of the Job Characteristics Model — the predecessor that Humphrey et al. (2007) replicated and extended. Research on job design, employee well-being (including curvilinear job-complexity → health effects), and career stage.

## Key Contributions

- **Fried & Ferris (1987):** meta-analytic validity evidence for JCT.
- **Fried et al. (2013):** curvilinear relation of job complexity to obesity indicators.
- **Oldham & Fried (2016):** review of past/present/future of job design.
