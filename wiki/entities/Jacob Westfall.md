# Jacob Westfall

Quantitative psychologist at the University of Texas at Austin (at time of 2017 publication). Collaborator with [[Tal Yarkoni]] on methodology reform, statistical power, and prediction in psychology.

## Key Contributions

- **Yarkoni & Westfall (2017):** "Choosing Prediction Over Explanation in Psychology" — see [[yarkoni-westfall-2017-prediction-vs-explanation]]
- Westfall, Kenny, & Judd (2014): Statistical power in experiments with stimulus samples
- Westfall & Yarkoni (2016): Statistically controlling for confounding constructs is harder than you think
