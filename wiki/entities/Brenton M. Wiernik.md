# Brenton M. Wiernik

Brenton M. Wiernik is a quantitative/personnel psychologist (University of South Florida at time of writing) known for psychometric meta-analysis and work on personality, careers, and research methods. Lead author of the PBCO meta-analysis that reframed protean/boundaryless orientations as a single **proactive career orientation** factor.
