# Stephen Broomell

Department of Social and Decision Sciences, Carnegie Mellon University.

## Key Contributions

- **Information-theoretic analysis of experience-based choice**: proved that standard experience stimuli cannot in principle estimate CPT parameters (Broomell & Bhatia 2014) — resolves description-experience gap debate
- **Risk communication and uncertainty**: work on communicating unknown unknowns (Broomell & Kane 2017)
- Decision modeling and measurement methodology
