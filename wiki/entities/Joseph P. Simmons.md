# Joseph P. Simmons

Behavioral scientist at The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. Co-author of [[simmons-et-al-2011-false-positive-psychology|False-Positive Psychology (2011)]], which coined [[Researcher Degrees of Freedom]] and helped launch the methodological reform wing of the [[Replication Crisis]]. With [[Leif D. Nelson]] and [[Uri Simonsohn]] he co-founded the Data Colada blog, an influential venue for forensic data critique and method development (e.g., p-curve).

## Key contributions

- Co-coined [[Researcher Degrees of Freedom]] and the disclosure-based remedy.
- Co-developer of p-curve analysis for detecting [[P-hacking]].
