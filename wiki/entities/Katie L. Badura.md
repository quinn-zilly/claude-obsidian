# Katie L. Badura

Nav: [[index]] | [[Leadership MOC]]

Management/IO scholar, Scheller College of Business, Georgia Institute of Technology. Studies leadership emergence, gender, and individual differences. Lead author of the [[badura-et-al-2022-leadership-emergence-integrative-review|integrative review of leadership emergence]] (JAP 2022) that introduced the [[Distal-Proximal Framework of Leader Emergence|distal–proximal framework]].
