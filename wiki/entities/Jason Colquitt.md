# Jason Colquitt

Industrial/organizational psychologist (University of Georgia, Terry College of Business); among the most influential **[[Organizational Justice|organizational justice]]** scholars.

## Contributions

- **Colquitt (2001)** — introduced the standard **four-factor justice scale** (procedural, distributive, interpersonal, informational), splitting "interactional justice" into interpersonal and informational facets. See [[Four Justice Dimensions]].
- **Colquitt et al. (2001)** — "Justice at the Millennium," the turn-of-millennium meta-analysis organizing the literature.
- Lead author, [[colquitt-et-al-2013-justice-millennium-decade-later|Colquitt et al. (2013)]] — the decade-later meta-analytic test of social exchange and affect lenses (493 samples).
- Co-author, [[colquitt-rodell-2015-measuring-justice-fairness|Colquitt & Rodell (2015)]] — the canonical justice measurement review and 2×2 [[Justice Measurement Taxonomy|taxonomy]].

Frequent collaborator with [[Jessica Rodell]].
