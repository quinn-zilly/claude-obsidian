# Joel Lefkowitz

Professor of Psychology at **Bernard M. Baruch College & the Graduate Center, CUNY**. Ph.D. (I/O specialization), Case Western Reserve, 1965. Founded and long headed CUNY's I/O Psychology doctoral program (created 1982).

Fellow of [[American Psychological Association|APA]], SIOP, and APS. Later career focused on **ethics and values in applied psychology**. Author of *Ethics and Values in Industrial-Organizational Psychology* (2003).

Best known in this wiki for [[lefkowitz-2008-values-organizational-psychology|Lefkowitz (2008)]], arguing I/O psychology should adopt a [[Scientist-Practitioner-Humanist (S-P-H) Model|Scientist-Practitioner-Humanist model]].
