# Juan Sanchez

Juan I. Sanchez — I/O psychologist, **Florida International University** (Knight-Ridder Byron Harless Chair in Management). Major contributor to **work/job analysis** theory, especially the reconceptualization of job analysis as work analysis and the reliability-vs-agreement debate. Long-time collaborator with [[Edward Levine]].

Key work in vault: [[Sanchez-Levine-2009-competency-modeling-vs-job-analysis]] — the six-dimension TJA-vs-CM framework ([[TJA vs CM (Six Dimensions)]]). Also cited across [[Garcia-Izquierdo-et-al-2015-work-analysis-personnel-selection]] and [[Singh-2008-Job-Analysis-Changing-Workplace]] (Sanchez 1994 scenario-based future KSAs).
