# Isabel Basson

Scientometrics researcher. Affiliated with the École de bibliothéconomie et des sciences de l'information, Université de Montréal, and the DST-NRF Centre of Excellence in Scientometrics and STI Policy / CREST, Stellenbosch University (South Africa).

First author and formal analyst on [[basson-et-al-2022-oa-dimensions-wos]], showing OA measurement depends on the bibliometric database used, with the largest divergence for the global South.

Also published on the OA citation advantage by WoS subject area (Scientometrics, 2021).
