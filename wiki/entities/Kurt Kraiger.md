# Kurt Kraiger

Kurt Kraiger is an industrial-organizational psychologist and leading scholar of workplace training and instruction. He is co-author (with J. Kevin Ford) of a major 2021 review synthesizing the science of workplace instruction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of [[kraiger-ford-2021-workplace-instruction]]: a comprehensive review of instructional science applied to workplace learning
- Work on training evaluation, learning outcomes taxonomy, and instructional design

## Affiliation

Professor of Psychology, Colorado State University (at time of Kraiger & Ford 2021 publication).
