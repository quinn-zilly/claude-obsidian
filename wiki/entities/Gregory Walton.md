# Gregory Walton

Social psychologist at **Stanford University** (Department of Psychology). Sole author of [[Walton-2014-Wise-Psychological-Interventions|Walton (2014)]], the article that named and synthesized [[Wise Interventions]]. Central figure in brief, precise, field-experimental social-psychological interventions in education.

Key work in the vault's scope: the [[Social Belonging Intervention]] (Walton & Cohen 2011), the noun-vs-verb voter-turnout study (Bryan, Walton, Rogers & Dweck 2011), and the "movie vs snapshot" framing of [[Recursive Processes (Wise Interventions)|recursive-process]] change. Frequent collaborators: [[Geoffrey Cohen]], [[Carol Dweck]], [[David Yeager]].
