# Deidra Schleicher

I/O psychologist and management scholar (Ivy College of Business, Iowa State University; formerly Texas A&M). Works on **performance management effectiveness**, appraisal reactions, and the measurement of PM.

Key contributions relevant to the wiki:
- Lead author of [[schleicher-2019-pm-effectiveness-review|Schleicher, Baumann, Sullivan & Yim (2019)]] — the integrative multilevel model of **[[PM Effectiveness Value Chains]]**.
- Schleicher et al. (2018, *Journal of Management*) — a systems taxonomy of PM *components* (the independent-variable companion to the 2019 criterion model).
- Schleicher & Day (1998) on frame-of-reference rater training.
