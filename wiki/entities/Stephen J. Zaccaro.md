# Stephen J. Zaccaro

Organizational psychologist; known for work on team leadership, functional leadership, and leader traits.

## Key Contributions
- **Marks, Zaccaro & Mathieu (2000)** [JAP, Table 1]: Leader briefing quality → shared mental models → team performance; effect stronger on novel tasks
- **Zaccaro, Rittman & Marks (2001)**: Team leadership framework — leader influences team performance through cognitive, motivational, and affective processes
- **Marks, Mathieu & Zaccaro (2001)**: Temporally-based taxonomy of team processes (see [[Teamwork Processes (Marks et al. Taxonomy)]])
- Co-author of [[lord-et-al-2017-leadership-in-applied-psychology]] (JAP 2017 centennial review)

## Research Focus
Team leadership, functional leadership, leader traits and attributes, multilevel leadership.
