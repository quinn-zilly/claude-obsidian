# Etienne LeBel

Etienne P. LeBel is a social psychologist and meta-scientist who developed the multi-dimensional credibility framework for evaluating scientific findings and built curatescience.org.

## Affiliation

University of Western Ontario (now Western University), Department of Psychology, London, Ontario, Canada.

## Key Contributions

- **[[lebel-2018-credibility-framework|"A Unified Framework to Quantify the Credibility of Scientific Findings" (2018, with Vanpaemel, Cheung, Campbell)]]** — *AMPPS* — Four-dimension framework (transparency, analytic reproducibility, analytic robustness, effect replicability) and the granular replication taxonomy (exact → very far)
- **curatescience.org** — crowdsourced platform for applying the credibility framework to published findings
- Contributor to [[open-science-collaboration-2015]] (one of 270 authors)
- Research on social cognition, implicit attitudes, and open science methodology

## Significance

LeBel's framework provides the *measurement vocabulary* for open science: rather than asking "did a study replicate (yes/no)?", it asks which of four credibility dimensions were assessed, and how each was scored. This reframing moves the conversation from binary replication to graded, multidimensional evaluation.
