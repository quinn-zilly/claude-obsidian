# Ryan D. Duffy

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work as a Calling MOC]]

Counseling / vocational psychologist (University of Maryland at the time of the 2009 paper; later University of Florida). With Bryan Dik, co-authored the founding definitional paper on calling and vocation, and has since produced a large empirical body on the antecedents and outcomes of living a calling.

## Key contributions

- **[[Dik-Duffy-2009-Calling-and-Vocation|Dik & Duffy (2009)]]** — calling as transcendent summons; calling/vocation distinction.
- Duffy & Dik (2013) — earlier review of the calling literature.
- Extensive empirical program (e.g., Living One's Calling Scale, Duffy et al. 2012a; work-volition as predictor/mediator of living a calling).
