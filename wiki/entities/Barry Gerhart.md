# Barry Gerhart

Influential researcher in strategic compensation and pay-for-performance.

**Affiliation (as of 2023):** Department of Management and Human Resources, Wisconsin School of Business, University of Wisconsin-Madison

---

## Key Contributions

- **[[fulmer-2023-compensation-performance]]** (with Fulmer & Kim, 2023) — 30-year review
- **Gerhart & Milkovich (1989, 1990, 1992)** — foundational strategic compensation research; "any direct or indirect payments to employees" definition; evidence that 1-pt rating advantage → 48% more promotions over 6 years
- **Gerhart & Rynes (2003)** — book-length treatment of compensation; incentive/sorting framework
- **Gerhart & Fang (2014, 2015)** — PFP and intrinsic motivation; finding that evidence does not support crowding-out hypothesis in organizational settings
- **Gerhart (2008)** — review of international compensation research
- **Gerhart (2017, 2023)** — comprehensive compensation textbook

---

## Research Focus

Strategic human resource management, compensation systems, pay-for-performance (incentive and sorting effects), cross-national compensation research, and the link between compensation design and organizational performance.
