# Elizabeth A. Janak

Researcher at the University at Albany (SUNY); co-author of the foundational critique of Kirkpatrick's training-evaluation levels.

## Key Work

- [[alliger-janak-1989-kirkpatrick-levels-thirty-years-later]]: with [[George M. Alliger]] — *Personnel Psychology* 42 — first systematic test of the three implicit assumptions behind [[Kirkpatrick Four-Level Model]]

## Contributions

Co-authored the 1989 review that challenged the assumed hierarchy and positive manifold of Kirkpatrick's four levels, establishing the empirical groundwork later expanded by [[alliger-et-al-1997-training-criteria-meta-analysis]].
