# Tomasz Obloj

Strategy scholar studying compensation, incentives, and the behavioral consequences of pay-for-performance systems.

**Affiliation (as of 2022):** HEC Paris, Jouy-en-Josas, France.

## Key contributions

- **[[obloj-zenger-2022-pay-transparency-equity-equality|Obloj & Zenger (2022, *Nature Human Behaviour*)]]** — large-N natural experiment showing pay transparency raises pay equity and equality while weakening the [[Performance Basis of Pay]].
- Earlier work (cited in the vault's [[Pay Transparency]] note) on how easy social comparisons under transparency can offset the incentive effects of performance-based [[Pay Dispersion|dispersion]].

## Focus

Incentives, gaming/behavioral responses to performance measures, pay transparency, and firm strategy.
