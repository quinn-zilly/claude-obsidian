# John F. Binning

I/O psychologist (Illinois State University). Co-author of the influential [[binning-barrett-1989-validity-personnel-decisions|Binning & Barrett (1989)]] conceptual analysis that unified construct/content/criterion validity into a single inferential framework for personnel decisions.
