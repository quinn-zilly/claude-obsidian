# Sanjay Srivastava

Nav: [[index]] | [[entities/_index]]

Personality psychologist, University of Oregon. Known for the functionalist account of personality structure ("the Five-Factor Model describes the structure of social perceptions"), the "set like plaster?" adult personality-development work (Srivastava, John, Gosling & Potter 2003), and research methods / open science. Co-author of [[Saucier-Srivastava-2015-structural-models-personality|Saucier & Srivastava (2015)]].
