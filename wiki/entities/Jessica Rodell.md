# Jessica Rodell

Industrial/organizational psychologist (University of Georgia); justice, fairness, and volunteering/citizenship researcher. Frequent collaborator with [[Jason Colquitt]].

## Contributions

- Co-author, [[colquitt-rodell-2015-measuring-justice-fairness|Colquitt & Rodell (2015)]] — justice measurement review and 2×2 [[Justice Measurement Taxonomy|taxonomy]].
- Co-author, [[colquitt-et-al-2013-justice-millennium-decade-later|Colquitt et al. (2013)]] — decade-later justice meta-analysis.
- Rodell & Colquitt (2009) — anticipatory justice in an organizational change context.
