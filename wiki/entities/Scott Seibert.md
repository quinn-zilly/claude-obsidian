# Scott Seibert

Scott E. Seibert is an organizational scholar in the Human Resource Management Department, School of Management and Labor Relations, **Rutgers University**. His work spans career success, proactive personality, career shocks, and social/human capital in careers (Seibert, Kraimer & Crant 2001; Seibert et al. 2013). Lead author of the 2024 critical review of contemporary career success.
