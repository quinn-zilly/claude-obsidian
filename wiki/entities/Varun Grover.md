# Varun Grover

## Role

**William S. Lee (Duke Energy) Distinguished Professor of Information Systems at Clemson University.** Among the most published and most cited IS researchers (200+ refereed publications; repeatedly ranked among the top IS researchers worldwide). Senior editor (emeritus) for *MIS Quarterly*, *Journal of the AIS*, and *Data Base*. Research areas: IT value, organizational impact, technostress.

## Key Contributions

- Senior co-author of [[roberts-2012-absorptive-capacity-is-review|Roberts et al. (2012)]] on [[Absorptive Capacity]] in IS research.
- Extensive work on IT capabilities and competitive advantage (e.g., Bhatt & Grover, 2005).
