# David Yeager

Developmental/social psychologist working on adolescent [[Wise Interventions]], especially [[Growth Mindset]] at scale. Co-author with [[Gregory Walton]] of "Social-psychological interventions in education: They're not magic" (Yeager & Walton 2011), the review emphasizing that these interventions work through psychological process and context — not magic. Also Yeager, Trzesniewski & Dweck (2013), an implicit-theories intervention reducing adolescent aggression. Cited in [[Walton-2014-Wise-Psychological-Interventions|Walton (2014)]].
