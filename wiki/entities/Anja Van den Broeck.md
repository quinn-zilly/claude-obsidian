# Anja Van den Broeck

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work Design (Concept)]]

Work/organizational psychologist at KU Leuven (Faculty of Economics and Business), also affiliated with the Optentia research unit at North West University. Co-author of [[parker-vandenbroeck-holman-2017-work-design-influences|Parker, Van den Broeck & Holman (2017)]] on the multilevel influences on work design.

Known for research on work motivation and the [[Job Demands-Resources Model|Job Demands-Resources model]] (e.g., Van den Broeck, De Cuyper, De Witte & Vansteenkiste, 2010, on the challenge–hindrance distinction in job demands) and on self-determination theory at work.
