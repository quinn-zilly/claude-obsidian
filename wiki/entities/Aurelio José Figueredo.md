# Aurelio José Figueredo

Quantitative psychologist / behavioral ecologist, University of Arizona. Co-author of [[Sechrest-Figueredo-1993-Program-Evaluation|Sechrest & Figueredo (1993)]]. His behavioral-ecology orientation supplies the paper's [[Ecological Paradigm in Evaluation|Newtonian-vs-Darwinian]] framing and its advocacy of multivariate techniques — including **sequential canonical analysis** as an exploratory form of path analysis (Gorsuch & Figueredo 1991). Frequent collaborator: [[Lee Sechrest]].
