# Robert G. Lord

Organizational psychologist; major contributor to social-cognitive approaches to leadership, particularly implicit leadership theories and leader categorization theory.

## Key Contributions
- **Lord, Foti & De Vader (1984)**: Leader categorization theory — leadership perception as prototype matching; categories are dynamic and context-sensitive
- **Lord, De Vader & Alliger (1986)** [JAP, Table 1]: First meta-analysis on leadership in JAP; revitalized trait perspective by attributing inconsistencies to sampling error
- **Lord (1977)**: Functional leadership behavior and social power
- Co-author of [[lord-et-al-2017-leadership-in-applied-psychology]] (JAP 2017 centennial review)

## Research Focus
Social-cognitive mechanisms underlying leadership perception; implicit theories; categorization processes; leadership and identity.
