# Ruth Wageman

Team-design and leadership scholar (then Amos Tuck School, Dartmouth). Research on interdependence, team design, and leadership development; long-time Hackman collaborator.

## Key Contributions (cited in wiki)
- Lead author, [[wageman-hackman-lehman-2005-team-diagnostic-survey|Wageman, Hackman & Lehman (2005)]] — the [[Team Diagnostic Survey]].
- Wageman (2001) — field-service team coaching study underpinning TDS coaching items.
- Hackman & Wageman (2005) — theory of team coaching.
