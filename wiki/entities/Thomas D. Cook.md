# Thomas D. Cook

Nav: [[index]] | [[log]]

Social scientist and evaluation methodologist. Co-author of Cook & Campbell (1979), *Quasi-Experimentation*, which first elaborated Campbell & Stanley's two-type scheme into the four-type [[Validity Typology]] (statistical conclusion, internal, construct, external). Co-author with [[William R. Shadish]] and Donald T. Campbell of the 2002 successor volume.
