# Nicholas Roberts

## Role

Information systems scholar at the **Johnson College of Business and Economics, University of South Carolina Upstate**. Ph.D. in Management Information Systems from Clemson University. Research interests: IT value, organizational learning, health IT.

- Lead author of [[roberts-2012-absorptive-capacity-is-review|Roberts et al. (2012)]] — the *MIS Quarterly* review synthesizing [[Absorptive Capacity]] in IS research.
