# Emma Bradshaw

SDT researcher; co-author of [[ryan-et-al-2022-sdt-meta-review]] (Ryan et al. 2022). Conducted meta-analyses on [[Goal Contents Theory]]: intrinsic aspirations → well-being (r = .24) and extrinsic relative centrality → well-being (r = −.22). Also meta-analysis on parental autonomy support → child well-being (r = .28; Bradshaw et al. 2021).
