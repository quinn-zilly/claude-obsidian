# Jeffery Shippmann

I-O psychologist and HR practitioner (PepsiCo, Inc.). **Chair of the SIOP Job Analysis and Competency Modeling Task Force (JACMTF)**, and lead author of [[Shippmann-et-al-2000-practice-of-competency-modeling|Shippmann et al. (2000), "The Practice of Competency Modeling"]] — the field's cardinal reference on competency modeling and its comparison to job analysis.

> [!note] Name spelling
> Appears as both "Shippmann" and "Schippmann" across the literature (e.g., Sackett et al. cite "Schippmann et al."). Same author.
