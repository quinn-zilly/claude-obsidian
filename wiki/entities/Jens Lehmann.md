# Jens Lehmann

Jens Lehmann is a computer scientist and the primary architect of the DBpedia project, which extracts structured knowledge from Wikipedia and publishes it as Linked Open Data.

## Affiliation

University of Leipzig and University of Bonn (Germany); also affiliated with the Fraunhofer Institute IAIS.

## Key Contributions

- **[[lehmann-2015-dbpedia|"DBpedia – A Large-scale, Multilingual Knowledge Base Extracted from Wikipedia" (2015)]]** — *Semantic Web* — Comprehensive description of DBpedia architecture, ontology, and applications
- Lead development of DBpedia extraction framework
- Research on ontology learning, knowledge graph completion, and question answering over linked data
