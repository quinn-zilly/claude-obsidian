# Michael Lounsbury

Organizational theorist at the University of Alberta. A leading figure in the institutional logics perspective and institutional theory more broadly. Frequent collaborator of [[Royston Greenwood]].

## Key Works in Wiki

- [[micelotta-2017-pathways-institutional-change]] — co-author; integrative review and typology (JOM 2017)

## Selected Other Works (not yet ingested)

- Thornton, Ocasio & Lounsbury (2012) — *The Institutional Logics Perspective* (Oxford University Press) — foundational book cited throughout the 2017 review
- Lounsbury & Glynn (2001) — "Cultural entrepreneurship: Stories, legitimacy, and the acquisition of resources" — key paper on cultural entrepreneurship / alignment pathway
- Lounsbury & Crumley (2007) — practice-based change via mutual fund industry example
- Schneiberg & Lounsbury (2008) — social movements and institutional analysis

## Research Focus

Institutional logics, cultural entrepreneurship, field-level dynamics, organizational theory and sociology.
