# Jos Akkermans

Jos Akkermans is an associate professor in the Department of Management and Organization, School of Business and Economics, **Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam**. He is a leading figure in the **career shocks** and **sustainable careers** literatures (Akkermans et al. 2018, 2021).
