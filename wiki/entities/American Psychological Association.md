# American Psychological Association (APA)

The largest scientific and professional organization of psychologists in the United States, headquartered in Washington, DC. Among its functions, it publishes and enforces the [[APA Ethics Code|Ethical Principles of Psychologists and Code of Conduct]] and the journal *American Psychologist*.

Membership commits members and student affiliates to comply with the [[apa-2017-ethical-principles-code-of-conduct|Ethics Code]]; the APA Ethics Committee may sanction violations up to termination of membership.
