# Aparna Joshi

Management scholar (Pennsylvania State University at time of the 2015 study) whose research centers on **gender inequality, diversity, and demographic dynamics in teams and organizations**. Her round-robin team studies (Joshi 2014) show gender operating as an **expertise cue**; Joshi & Knight (2015) extend the interactional-dynamics account.
