# Jeffery A. Thompson

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work as a Calling MOC]]

Organizational scholar (Romney Institute of Public Management, Brigham Young University). With Stuart Bunderson, developed the **neoclassical** conception of calling and, later, the integrative **[[Transcendent Calling|transcendent calling]]** framework.

## Key contributions

- **Bunderson & Thompson (2009)** — the zookeeper study; neoclassical calling scale; calling's "double-edged sword" (moral duty, sacrifice, org criticism).
- **[[Thompson-Bunderson-2019-Work-as-a-Calling|Thompson & Bunderson (2019)]]** — *Annual Review* taxonomy; inner × outer requiredness 2×2; transcendent calling.
