# Krista Uggerslev

I-O psychologist (Northern Alberta Institute of Technology / University of Manitoba at time of study). Recruitment and applicant-reactions researcher; co-author of the influential **Chapman et al. (2005)** recruiting meta-analysis. Lead author of [[uggerslev-et-al-2012-recruiting-through-stages|Uggerslev, Fassina & Kraichy (2012)]], the meta-analytic test of recruiting predictors across stages.
