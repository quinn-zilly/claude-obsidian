# Daniel Levinthal

## Role

Strategy and organization-theory scholar at **The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania**. Co-originator of **[[Absorptive Capacity]]** and a central figure in [[Exploration vs Exploitation|exploration/exploitation]] learning theory.

## Key Contributions

- **Cohen & Levinthal (1989, 1990, 1994)** — the absorptive-capacity trilogy (with [[Wesley Cohen]]).
- **Levinthal & March (1993)** — "The Myopia of Learning" — the exploration/exploitation balancing problem.

## Wiki Sources

- Referenced throughout [[roberts-2012-absorptive-capacity-is-review|Roberts et al. (2012)]] as the construct's origin.
