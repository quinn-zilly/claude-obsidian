# Joep Cornelissen

## Role in This Wiki

Corresponding author of [[cornelissen-werner-2014-framing-review]] — the definitive multi-level review of framing in management and organizational theory. Professor at VU University Amsterdam at time of publication; subsequently at Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University.

## Key Contributions

- **Framing review (with Werner 2014):** Academy of Management Annals review integrating micro, meso, and macro framing literatures; argues for the priming/framing distinction and a multi-level research agenda.
- **Conceptual blending** (Cornelissen & Clarke 2010): how managers blend cognitive frames through analogy to generate new strategic understanding; builds on Fauconnier & Turner (2002).
- **Corporate communication and rhetoric:** research on how organizations use language, metaphor, and narrative to construct organizational identity and manage legitimacy.
- **Interaction analysis** (Cornelissen, Mantere & Vaara 2014): micro-level study of police communication in a failed anti-terrorist operation; demonstrates framing dynamics in real-time interaction.
