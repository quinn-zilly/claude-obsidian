# Dorothy Leidner

## Role

Information Systems scholar; at Baylor University at the time of the 2006 *MIS Quarterly* culture review. A recurring author in this vault's IS cluster.

## Key contributions

- **[[leidner-kayworth-2006-culture-is-review]]** (with [[Timothy Kayworth]]) — integrative review of IT-culture research and the theory of [[IT-Culture Conflict]].
- **[[alavi-leidner-2001-knowledge-management]]** (with [[Maryam Alavi]]) — foundational conceptual framework for knowledge management and KM systems.
- Earlier empirical work cited in the review (e.g., Leidner et al. 1999 on EIS use across Mexican, Swedish, and US managers; Jarvenpaa & Leidner 1998).

## Themes

Cross-cultural and organizational influences on IS, knowledge management systems, executive information systems, and the values groups attribute to IT.
