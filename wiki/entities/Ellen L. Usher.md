# Ellen L. Usher

Nav: [[index]] | [[Social Cognitive Theory]]

Educational psychologist (University of Kentucky), researcher on the **[[Sources of Self-Efficacy|sources of academic self-efficacy]]** and their measurement, with attention to gender and demographic differences in how students weight efficacy information. Co-author of **[[schunk-usher-2012-social-cognitive-theory-motivation|Social Cognitive Theory and Motivation (2012)]]** with [[Dale H. Schunk]].
