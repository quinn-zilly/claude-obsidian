# CMU Eberly Center

**Full name:** Eberly Center for Teaching Excellence & Educational Innovation
**Institution:** Carnegie Mellon University
**Location:** Tepper Quad, Suite 1310, Pittsburgh, PA 15213
**Website:** [cmu.edu/teaching](https://www.cmu.edu/teaching)

## What It Does

Research and consulting center for higher education pedagogy at CMU. Produces research-grounded teaching resources, consultation services, fellowships, and the GAITAR initiative (generative AI + education).

## Key Output: Learning Principles

Published a synthesis of seven research-based [[cmu-eberly-learning-principles|learning principles]] distilled from cognitive science, motivational psychology, and educational research. Widely cited in instructor development contexts.

## Notable Initiatives

- **GAITAR:** Generative AI + Teaching and Research initiative; includes position statements, faculty institutes, student learning modules.
- **Future Faculty Program:** Graduate student teaching development.
- **Consultation Services:** Direct support for CMU instructors.
