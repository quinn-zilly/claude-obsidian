# Maryam Alavi

## Role

John and Lucy Cook Chair of Information Technology at the **Goizueta Business School, Emory University**. Prominent IS researcher specializing in knowledge management systems, IT-mediated organizational learning, and the role of technology in organizational knowledge processes.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of the most-cited KMS conceptual paper: Alavi & Leidner (2001), *MIS Quarterly*
- Process view of knowledge management — knowledge creation, storage/retrieval, transfer, application
- Framework for understanding knowledge through multiple perspectives (state of mind, object, process, access, capability)
- Research on computer-mediated communication and organizational memory systems
