# Mladen Adamovic

Organizational-behaviour researcher at the Department of Human Resource Management & Employment Relations, **King's Business School, King's College London**. Works on **[[Organizational Justice|organizational justice]]**, workforce **diversity/dissimilarity**, **cross-cultural** justice, virtual work, and migrant/refugee integration.

## Contributions

- Author, [[adamovic-2023-organizational-justice-review|Adamovic (2023)]] — semi-systematic review synthesizing the justice field and setting a research agenda (cultural sensitivity, diversity, justice–conflict, changing nature of work).
- Empirical work on the cultural influence on reward-allocation preferences across 28 countries (2023); procedural-justice climate and migrant-worker engagement; ethnic discrimination in recruitment and leadership access (field experiments).
