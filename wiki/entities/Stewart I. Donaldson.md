# Stewart I. Donaldson

Evaluation scholar; author of *Program Theory-Driven Evaluation Science* (2007). Described four sources of [[Program Theory|program theory]] (prior theory/research, implicit theories, observation, exploratory research) and stressed that TDE is method-neutral. Prominent advocate for the causal-explanation value of [[Theory-Driven Evaluation]].
