# David Dunning

Nav: [[index]] | [[Dunning-Kruger Effect]]

Social psychologist (Cornell, later University of Michigan), senior author of **[[kruger-dunning-1999-unskilled-and-unaware|"Unskilled and Unaware of It" (1999)]]**. Broad program on **flawed self-assessment**, motivated reasoning, and the limits of introspection (e.g., the "Dunning-Kruger" line of work on how the incompetent lack the metacognitive skill to recognize their incompetence).
