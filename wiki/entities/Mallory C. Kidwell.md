# Mallory C. Kidwell

Psychologist (University of Utah). Lead author of Kidwell et al. (2016), the badge-effect study showing open-data/open-materials badges at *Psychological Science* increased sharing — a key reform-efficacy result cited throughout the prevalence literature. Co-author and double-coder on [[hardwicke-et-al-2022-transparency-prevalence-psychology|Hardwicke et al. (2022)]].
