# Deborah Rupp

Industrial/organizational psychologist; a principal architect of the **[[Multifoci Justice & Target Similarity|multifoci approach]]** to [[Organizational Justice|organizational justice]] (justice organized by accountable *source*, not just *type*). Also known for behavioral ethics, assessment centers, and CSR.

## Contributions

- Lead author, [[rupp-et-al-2014-multifoci-justice-meta-analysis|Rupp, Shao, Jones & Liao (2014)]] — the meta-analytic case for multifoci justice, mediated by source-specific [[Social Exchange Theory|social exchange]].
- Co-developer of the multifoci / multiple-needs models with [[Russell Cropanzano]] (Cropanzano, Byrne, Bobocel & Rupp 2001; Rupp & Cropanzano 2002; Rupp & Paddock 2010).
- Long-running program on third-party justice, deonance, and CSR perceptions.
