# Wolf Vanpaemel

Researcher at KU Leuven (Belgium) specializing in mathematical psychology and Bayesian methods. Co-author of the unified credibility framework for scientific findings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author (with [[Etienne LeBel]], Irene Cheung, and Lorne Campbell) of [[lebel-2018-credibility-framework|"A Unified Framework to Quantify the Credibility of Scientific Findings"]] (2018, *AMPPS*).
