# Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC)

US federal agency enforcing **Title VII of the Civil Rights Act (1964)** and other anti-discrimination employment law. With the Civil Service Commission, Department of Labor, and Department of Justice, jointly issued the [[EEOC-1978-uniform-guidelines-employee-selection]] — the governing federal framework for the proper use of employment tests and selection procedures (codified 29 CFR 1607 / 41 CFR 60-3).
