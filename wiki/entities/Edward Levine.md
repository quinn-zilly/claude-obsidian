# Edward Levine

Edward L. Levine — I/O psychologist, **University of South Florida**. Long-time collaborator with [[Juan Sanchez]] on job/work analysis theory. Co-author of *Job Analysis* (Brannick, Levine & Morgeson 2007), the work-analysis critique [[Sanchez-Levine-2009-competency-modeling-vs-job-analysis]], and the field-reframing review [[Sanchez-Levine-2012-rise-fall-job-analysis|Sanchez & Levine (2012)]].
