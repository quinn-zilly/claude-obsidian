# David V. Day

Organizational psychologist; known for work on LMX, leadership development, and team leadership capacity.

## Key Contributions
- **Gerstner & Day (1997)** [JAP, Table 1]: First comprehensive meta-analysis of LMX literature; identified low leader–member agreement on LMX quality
- **Day, Gronn & Salas (2004)**: Leadership capacity in teams; collective leadership capacity
- Co-author of [[lord-et-al-2017-leadership-in-applied-psychology]] (JAP 2017 centennial review)

## Research Focus
Leader-member exchange, leadership development, team leadership capacity, longitudinal and multilevel approaches.
