# Gerald V. Barrett

I/O psychologist (University of Akron), known for work on validity, personnel selection, and content validation, and as an expert witness in employment litigation. Co-author of the [[binning-barrett-1989-validity-personnel-decisions|Binning & Barrett (1989)]] unified validity framework; also argued against uncritical "dust-bowl empiricism" and for the theoretical grounding of validity evidence (Barrett, Alexander, O'Connor & Forbes 1978).
