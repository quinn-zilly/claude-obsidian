# Daan van Knippenberg

Organizational-behavior professor (Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University; later Drexel). Known for work on team diversity, leadership, and creativity.

## Key Contributions (cited in wiki)
- Coauthor, [[mathieu-et-al-2017-century-work-teams|Mathieu et al. (2017)]].
- Originator (with De Dreu & Homan, 2004) of the **[[Categorization-Elaboration Model]]** — the backbone of the [[homan-et-al-2020-leading-diversity-functional|LeaD]] diversity-leadership theory.
