# Dale H. Schunk

Nav: [[index]] | [[Social Cognitive Theory]]

Educational psychologist (UNC Greensboro), a leading researcher on **self-efficacy in academic settings**, self-regulated learning, and modeling. Extensive program of studies on cognitive modeling, coping vs. mastery models, goal setting, and progress feedback in children's learning. Co-author of **[[schunk-usher-2012-social-cognitive-theory-motivation|Social Cognitive Theory and Motivation (2012)]]** with [[Ellen L. Usher]].
