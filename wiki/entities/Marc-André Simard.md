# Marc-André Simard

Scientometrics researcher, Université de Montréal. Co-author of [[basson-et-al-2022-oa-dimensions-wos]].

Lead author of Simard et al. (ISSI 2021), "Geographic Differences in the Uptake of Open Access" — the WoS–Unpaywall linkage method that the Basson et al. paper reuses for WoS OA status.
