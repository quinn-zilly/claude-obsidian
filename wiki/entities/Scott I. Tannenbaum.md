# Scott I. Tannenbaum

I-O psychologist and consultant; affiliated with Executive Consulting Group, Inc. and the University at Albany Department of Management.

## Key Work

- Tannenbaum & Yukl (1992): "Training and Development in Work Organizations" — *Annual Review of Psychology* 43
- Tannenbaum & Woods (1992): Evaluation strategy under organizational constraints
- Mathieu, Tannenbaum & Salas (1993): Individual/situational influences on training effectiveness
- [[alliger-et-al-1997-training-criteria-meta-analysis]]: Co-author

## Contributions

Broad contributor to training effectiveness, transfer climate, and trainee expectations research. The Tracey, Tannenbaum & Kavanaugh (1995) study established the importance of work environment (transfer climate) as a determinant of training application.
