# Eun-Suk Lee

Nav: [[index]] | [[Organizational Identification (Outcomes)]]

KAIST College of Business. first author of **[[lee-park-koo-2015-organizational-identification-meta-analysis|"Identifying Organizational Identification as a Basis for Attitudes and Behaviors" (2015, *Psychological Bulletin*)]]**, a meta-analysis positioning organizational identification within the attitude–behavior framework and testing national-culture moderation.
