# Daniel R. Ilgen

Psychology and management professor, Michigan State University. Influential teams and motivation scholar.

## Key Contributions (cited in wiki)
- Coauthor, [[mathieu-et-al-2017-century-work-teams|Mathieu et al. (2017)]].
- Ilgen, Hollenbeck, Johnson & Jundt (2005) — the **[[IPO–IMOI Framework|IMOI]]** update replacing the linear IPO model.
- Kozlowski & Ilgen (2006) — team definition and effectiveness review.
