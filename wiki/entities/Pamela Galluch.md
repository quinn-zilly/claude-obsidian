# Pamela Galluch

## Role

Information systems scholar at **Roanoke College**. Ph.D. from Clemson University. Research examines ICT characteristics' influence on stress and coping behaviors, attention with IT, maladaptive Internet use, and absorptive capacity.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of [[roberts-2012-absorptive-capacity-is-review|Roberts et al. (2012)]] on [[Absorptive Capacity]] in IS research.
