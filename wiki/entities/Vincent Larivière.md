# Vincent Larivière

Prominent scientometrics researcher, École de bibliothéconomie et des sciences de l'information, Université de Montréal. Senior author on [[basson-et-al-2022-oa-dimensions-wos]] (conceptualization, methodology, funding, resources).

Major figure in OA measurement and bibliometrics: co-author of the Piwowar et al. (2018) large-scale OA prevalence study (origin of the Unpaywall five-category scheme used here), *Measuring Research* (2018, with [[Cassidy R. Sugimoto]]), and funder-mandate compliance work (Nature, 2018).
