# Ernest C. Davenport Jr.

Quantitative/educational psychologist (Department of Educational Psychology, University of Minnesota). With Mark L. Davison, co-originated **criterion profile analysis** — the regression-based level/pattern decomposition later extended to meta-analytic data.

- [[davison-davenport-2002-criterion-related-patterns|Davison & Davenport (2002)]] — the primary CPA paper.
