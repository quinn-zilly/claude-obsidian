# Alice H. Eagly

Social psychologist; leading researcher on gender and leadership, role congruity theory, and meta-analytic methods in social psychology.

## Key Contributions
- **Eagly & Johnson (1990)**: Meta-analysis of gender and leadership style — women more democratic/participative
- **Eagly & Karau (1991)**: Gender and leader emergence meta-analysis
- **Eagly, Makhijani & Klonsky (1992)**: Gender and evaluation bias meta-analysis — women devalued for agentic styles
- **Eagly, Karau & Makhijani (1995)**: Gender and leader effectiveness meta-analysis — context effects
- **Eagly & Karau (2002)**: Role congruity theory — framework explaining prejudice toward female leaders
- **Eagly, Johannesen-Schmidt & van Engen (2003)**: Women more transformational; meta-analysis
- **Koenig, Eagly et al. (2011)**: Leadership prototype masculinity has ebbed; meta-analysis
- Co-author of [[lord-et-al-2017-leadership-in-applied-psychology]] (JAP 2017 centennial review)

## Research Focus
Gender and leadership; stereotypes and prejudice; social role theory; role congruity theory; meta-analytic methods.
