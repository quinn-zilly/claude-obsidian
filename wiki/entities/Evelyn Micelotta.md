# Evelyn Micelotta

Organizational theorist at the University of New Mexico. Works at the intersection of institutional theory, institutional logics, and organizational change.

## Key Works in Wiki

- [[micelotta-2017-pathways-institutional-change]] — lead author; integrative review and typology of institutional change pathways (JOM 2017)
- Greenwood, Raynard, Kodeih, Micelotta & Lounsbury (2011) — "Institutional complexity and organizational responses," *Academy of Management Annals* (cited extensively in the 2017 paper; not yet ingested)

## Research Focus

Institutional change processes, institutional complexity, organizational responses to multiple logics.
