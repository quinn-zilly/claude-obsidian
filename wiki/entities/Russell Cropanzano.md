# Russell Cropanzano

Organizational psychologist; major theorist of **[[Social Exchange Theory]]**, **[[Organizational Justice|justice]]**, and **affect at work**.

## Contributions

- Co-author, [[cropanzano-mitchell-2005-social-exchange-theory|Cropanzano & Mitchell (2005)]] — the canonical modern SET review (three-pillar framework: rules, resources, relationships).
- Co-originator of **[[Affective Events Theory]]** (Weiss & Cropanzano 1996) — already in the vault; links work events → affect → behavior.
- Developed "**contemporary social exchange theory**" in justice research (Cropanzano & Byrne 2000; Cropanzano & Rupp 2008; Cropanzano, Rupp et al. 2001) — the exchange-quality mediators (trust, commitment, POS, LMX).
- Editor, *The Oxford Handbook of Justice in the Workplace* (Cropanzano & Ambrose 2015), which contains [[colquitt-rodell-2015-measuring-justice-fairness|Colquitt & Rodell's measurement chapter]].

- Co-author, [[cropanzano-bowen-gilliland-2007-management-organizational-justice|Cropanzano, Bowen & Gilliland (2007)]] — the practitioner-facing "management of organizational justice" (five HR systems; [[Fair Process Effect]]; [[Due Process Approach to Appraisal]]).
- Co-originator of the **[[Multifoci Justice & Target Similarity|multifoci / multiple-needs]]** models (Cropanzano, Byrne, Bobocel & Rupp 2001) with [[Deborah Rupp]].
