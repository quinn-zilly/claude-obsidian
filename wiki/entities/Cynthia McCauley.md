# Cynthia D. McCauley

Senior Fellow at the **Center for Creative Leadership (CCL)**. Major contributor to experience-based leadership development research and practice.

## Key Works
- McCauley (2006). *Developmental Assignments: Creating Learning Experiences Without Changing Jobs*. CCL.
- McCauley, DeRue, Yost & Taylor (2014). *Experience-Driven Leader Development*. Wiley.
- McCauley & McCall (2014). *Using Experience to Develop Leadership Talent*. Jossey-Bass.
- **McCauley & Yost (2021)** — [[mccauley-yost-2021-comfort-zone]] — "Stepping to the Edge of One's Comfort Zone" in *The Age of Agility*

## Contributions
- Built practical frameworks for categorizing stretch experiences / developmental assignments
- Co-developed the experience-driven development model connecting stretch to organizational systems
- Coauthor with [[Paul Yost]] on learning agility and stretch experience strategies
