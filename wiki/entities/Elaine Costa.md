# Elaine Costa

Researcher at the **McIntire School of Commerce, University of Virginia**. Studies discrimination, diversity/equity/inclusion, and the design and evaluation of bias-reduction interventions.

Author of [[costa-2024-discrimination-interventions|Costa (2024)]], a *JAP* meta-analysis introducing an **[[Attitude Dimension Consistency|attitude dimension consistency]]** framework and a **[[Bias Intervention Taxonomy|taxonomy]]** of bias interventions, finding accountability and social-norm interventions most effective at reducing workplace discrimination.
