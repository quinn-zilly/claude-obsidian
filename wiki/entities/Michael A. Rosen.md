# Michael A. Rosen

Researcher on teams, team cognition, and expert team performance; frequent Salas collaborator (later prominent in healthcare/patient-safety team science at Johns Hopkins).

## Key Contributions (cited in wiki)
- [[salas-et-al-2009-wisdom-of-collectives|Salas, Rosen, Burke & Goodwin (2009)]] — update of teamwork competencies (ABCs, Big Five).
- Salas, Rosen, Burke, Goodwin & Fiore (2006) — "The making of a dream team," source of the mechanisms of expert team performance (Table 3.2).
