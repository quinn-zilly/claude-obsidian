# Uri Simonsohn

Behavioral scientist (then at The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania). Co-author of [[simmons-et-al-2011-false-positive-psychology|False-Positive Psychology (2011)]] and co-founder of the Data Colada blog with [[Joseph P. Simmons]] and [[Leif D. Nelson]]. Known for forensic detection of data fabrication and for developing p-curve analysis.

## Key contributions

- Co-coined [[Researcher Degrees of Freedom]].
- Lead developer of p-curve analysis for detecting [[P-hacking]] and evidential value.
