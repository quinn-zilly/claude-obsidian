# Jason D. Marshall

Management scholar at **Creighton University** (Heider College of Business). Lead author of the [[marshall-aguinis-beltran-2024-theories-of-performance|2024 integrative review of performance theories]] that introduced the [[CORE Model of Performance]]. Project grew out of the PhD Project / Management Faculty of Color Association's Research Collaboration Initiative.
