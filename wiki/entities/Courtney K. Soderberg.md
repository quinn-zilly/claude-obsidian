# Courtney K. Soderberg

Methodologist/statistician affiliated with the [[Center for Open Science]]. Lead author and primary analyst of [[soderberg-et-al-2021-registered-reports-quality|Soderberg et al. (2021)]], the first peer-perception comparison of [[Registered Reports]] vs the standard publishing model.
