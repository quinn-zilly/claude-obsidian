# Geoffrey Cohen

Social psychologist (Stanford), a leading developer of self-integrity–based [[Wise Interventions]]. Principal architect of [[Values Affirmation]] interventions (Cohen, Garcia, Purdie-Vaughns, Apfel & Brzustoski 2009) and co-author with [[Gregory Walton]] of the [[Social Belonging Intervention]] (Walton & Cohen 2011). Work centers on self-affirmation, [[Recursive Processes (Wise Interventions)|recursive]] threat cycles, and closing achievement gaps. Cited throughout [[Walton-2014-Wise-Psychological-Interventions|Walton (2014)]].
