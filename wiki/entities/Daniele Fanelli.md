# Daniele Fanelli

Meta-scientist studying bias, misconduct, and the integrity of the scientific literature through large-scale bibliometric and content analysis. At the time of [[fanelli-2012-negative-results-disappearing|Fanelli (2012)]] he was at ISSTI, University of Edinburgh.

## Contributions in this Vault

- [[fanelli-2012-negative-results-disappearing|Negative Results Are Disappearing (2012)]] — first direct evidence of rising [[Positive-Outcome Bias]].
- Known for the "hierarchy of the sciences" line of work (Fanelli 2010): positive-result rates rise from hard to soft sciences.
