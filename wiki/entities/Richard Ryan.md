# Richard M. Ryan

Psychologist; co-developer of [[Self-Determination Theory]] with [[Edward Deci]] at University of Rochester.

Primary contributions: SDT framework, [[Cognitive Evaluation Theory]], organismic integration, psychological needs and well-being. Co-author of foundational 1985 book *Intrinsic Motivation and Self-Determination in Human Behavior* (with Deci).

Key papers: Ryan & Connell 1989 (autonomy continuum), Ryan & Deci 2000 ([[ryan-deci-2000-sdt]]), Ryan et al. 1999 (Russian cross-cultural), Ryan et al. 2022 ([[ryan-et-al-2022-sdt-meta-review]] — meta-review of 60+ SDT meta-analyses).

Applied SDT across health care, education, sport, religion, psychotherapy.
