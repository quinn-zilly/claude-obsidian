# Justin Kruger

Nav: [[index]] | [[Dunning-Kruger Effect]]

Social psychologist; as a graduate student with [[David Dunning]] at Cornell, co-authored the 1999 paper **[[kruger-dunning-1999-unskilled-and-unaware|"Unskilled and Unaware of It"]]** that gave rise to the **[[Dunning-Kruger Effect]]**. Later at NYU Stern; research on judgment, egocentrism in social comparison, and self-assessment.
