# Edwin A. Locke

Nav: [[index]]

Industrial–organizational psychologist. R. H. Smith School of Business, University of Maryland. Co-developer (with [[Gary Latham]]) of [[Goal-Setting Theory]] over 35+ years.

## Key Contributions

- Formulated GST inductively from empirical research beginning in the 1960s
- Established difficulty–performance linear function (vs. Atkinson's curvilinear prediction)
- Identified the four goal mechanisms: direction, effort, persistence, strategy
- Introduced "motivation hub" concept (personal goals + self-efficacy = proximal causes)
- Co-developed the [[High-Performance Cycle]] model
- Demonstrated goals + self-efficacy mediate personality effects on performance

## Major Works

- Locke & Latham (1984) — *Goal Setting: A Motivational Technique That Works!*
- Locke & Latham (1990) — *A Theory of Goal Setting and Task Performance*
