# Linda Argote

## Role

Leading organizational learning researcher at the **Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University**. Co-director of the Center for Organizational Learning, Innovation and Knowledge (COLIK). One of the most cited scholars in the organizational learning literature.

## Key Contributions

- Foundational empirical work on organizational learning curves (Argote & Epple, 1990) — including the Lockheed L-1011 case demonstrating knowledge depreciation
- Theoretical framework: **experience × context → knowledge** (Argote & Miron-Spektor, 2011)
- Decomposition of organizational learning into four processes: search, knowledge creation, knowledge retention, knowledge transfer (Argote, Lee & Park, 2021)
- Conceptualization of distributed knowledge repositories: members, tools, routines, transactive memory systems
