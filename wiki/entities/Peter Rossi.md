# Peter Rossi

Sociologist and evaluation methodologist (Peter H. Rossi). Named in [[Sechrest-Figueredo-1993-Program-Evaluation|Sechrest & Figueredo (1993)]] among the field's early leaders and, with [[Huey-Tsyh Chen]], a principal proponent of [[Theory-Driven Evaluation]] (Chen & Rossi 1987, 1989). Source of the near-"metallic law" the authors quote on the discouraging nature of [[Formative vs Summative Evaluation|summative]] impact assessment: "the expected value of the outcome of an impact assessment is zero or close to it." Co-author of the widely used *Evaluation: A Systematic Approach* (Rossi & Freeman).
