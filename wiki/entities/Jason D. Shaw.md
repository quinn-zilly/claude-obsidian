# Jason D. Shaw

Compensation and HR scholar; a central figure in the **[[Pay Dispersion]]** literature. At time of the review, Department of Management and Marketing, Faculty of Business, Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

His body of work (Shaw, Gupta & Delery 2002; Shaw & Gupta 2007; Kepes, Delery & Gupta 2009; Shaw et al. 2009) established the **[[Explained vs Unexplained Pay Dispersion]]** decomposition that organizes the field.
