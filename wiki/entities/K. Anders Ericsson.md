# K. Anders Ericsson

Swedish-born psychologist (1947–2020), Florida State University. The central figure in modern **expertise research** and originator of the [[Deliberate Practice]] framework (Ericsson, Krampe & Tesch-Römer 1993). Argued that expert performance is overwhelmingly *acquired* through structured practice rather than innate [[Talent vs Deliberate Practice|talent]].

Key contributions in this vault: co-author of [[ericsson-lehmann-1996-expert-exceptional-performance|Ericsson & Lehmann (1996)]]; developer of [[Skilled Memory Theory]] (with [[William G. Chase]], 1982) and [[Long-Term Working Memory]] (with Kintsch, 1995); co-author of *Protocol Analysis* (with [[Herbert A. Simon]]).
