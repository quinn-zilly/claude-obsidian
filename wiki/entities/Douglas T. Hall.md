# Douglas T. Hall

Douglas T. "Tim" Hall is a professor at the **Questrom School of Business, Boston University** and the originator of the **protean career** concept (Hall 1976, 1996, 2002, 2004). His decades of work established self-direction, intrinsic values orientation, and psychological success as the core of protean career theory, and (with Briscoe) the Protean Career Attitudes Scale.
