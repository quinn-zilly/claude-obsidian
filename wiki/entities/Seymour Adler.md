# Seymour Adler

I-O psychologist and practitioner (Aon Hewitt). Chaired the 2015 SIOP debate "Getting Rid of Performance Ratings: Genius or Folly?" and led the resulting focal article. Argues on the **pro-ratings** side: performance is always evaluated in some form and differentiated evaluation has real value.

Lead author, [[adler-et-al-2016-getting-rid-of-ratings|Adler et al. (2016)]].
