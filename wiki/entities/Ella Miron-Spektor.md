# Ella Miron-Spektor

Ella Miron-Spektor is an organizational behavior and management scholar specializing in learning, creativity, and team dynamics.

## Affiliation

Bar-Ilan University (Faculty of Business Administration), Ramat Gan, Israel.

## Key Contributions

- **[[argote-miron-spektor-2011-org-learning-journal|"Organizational Learning: From Experience to Knowledge" (2011, with Linda Argote)]]** — *Organization Science* — Developed the active/latent context distinction within Argote's experience-to-knowledge framework
- Research on **paradoxical thinking** in teams — how teams that embrace tensions between exploration and exploitation achieve higher creativity
- Studies on **learning from errors** and psychological safety in team contexts

## Research Focus

Miron-Spektor bridges organizational learning theory with creativity and innovation research. Her contribution to the Argote framework is the systematic treatment of *context* — distinguishing what is currently salient in the environment (active context) from what is residually accumulated (latent context).
