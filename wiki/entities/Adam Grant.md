# Adam Grant

Nav: [[index]] | [[Relational Job Design]]

Organizational psychologist at The Wharton School (University of Pennsylvania). Originator of **[[Relational Job Design]]** (Grant, 2007, *Academy of Management Review*) — designing the relational architecture of jobs to increase the motivation to make a prosocial difference by connecting employees to the beneficiaries of their work. Extended it empirically in "The significance of task significance" (Grant, 2008, *JAP*).

Author of the bestselling *Give and Take* (2013), a practitioner version of the relational work design approach.

> [!warning] Not to be confused with
> Distinct from [[Robert Grant]] (strategy scholar) elsewhere in this vault. The relational job design work is **Adam M. Grant**.
