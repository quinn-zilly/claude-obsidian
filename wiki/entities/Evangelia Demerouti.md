# Evangelia Demerouti

Nav: [[index]] | [[Job Demands-Resources Model]]

Work and organizational psychologist; lead author of the foundational **[[Job Demands-Resources Model]]** paper (Demerouti, Bakker, Nachreiner & Schaufeli, 2001, *Journal of Applied Psychology*), "The job demands-resources model of burnout." The JD-R model generalized Karasek's demand-control model — any resource (not just control) can buffer demands — and added a dual path linking demands to burnout and resources to engagement.

Long collaboration with Arnold Bakker; central figure in the burnout/engagement and work design literatures.
