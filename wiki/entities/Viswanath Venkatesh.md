# Viswanath Venkatesh

Information systems researcher, lead author of the original [[UTAUT]] (Venkatesh, Morris, Davis & Davis, 2003, *MIS Quarterly*) and UTAUT2 (Venkatesh, Thong & Xu, 2012). Also co-developed TAM2 (Venkatesh & Davis 2000) and TAM3 (Venkatesh & Bala 2008) in the [[Technology Acceptance Model|TAM]] lineage.

In this vault he appears as the originator of the model that [[dwivedi-2019-utaut-revision|Dwivedi et al.]] critique and revise.
