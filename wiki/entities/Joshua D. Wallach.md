# Joshua D. Wallach

Epidemiologist (Yale School of Public Health). Lead author of the biomedicine transparency-prevalence sweep (Wallach et al. 2018) that the psychology indicator battery is modeled on; co-author and data coder on [[hardwicke-et-al-2022-transparency-prevalence-psychology|Hardwicke et al. (2022)]]. Frequent [[John Ioannidis]] collaborator on [[Meta-Research]].
