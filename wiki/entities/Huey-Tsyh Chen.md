# Huey-Tsyh Chen

Evaluation theorist whose 1990 book *Theory-Driven Evaluations* consolidated [[Theory-Driven Evaluation]] as a recognized approach. Advocates a stakeholder-oriented approach to [[Program Theory|program-theory]] formulation (evaluator as facilitator), nonlinear/ecological program-theory models (action model + change model, 2005), and the method-neutrality of TDE. Distinguished four variants of TDE and argued that black-box evaluation fails to give decision-makers explanatory knowledge (the "new drug" analogy).
