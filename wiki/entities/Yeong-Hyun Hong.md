# Yeong-Hyun Hong

HR/organizational researcher studying employee benefits, social exchange, and employee attitudes.

**Affiliation (as of 2024):** Department of Management Studies, University of Michigan–Dearborn.

**ORCID:** 0000-0002-0243-8175

## Key contributions

- **[[hong-ford-jong-2024-employee-benefits-meta-analysis|Hong, Ford & Jong (2024, *Journal of Applied Psychology*)]]** — lead author of the first meta-analysis separating benefit availability, use, and subjective evaluation (the [[Benefit Experience Framework]]); led formal analysis and project administration.

## Focus

Employee benefits, [[Perceived Organizational Support|POS]], social exchange, employee well-being and attitudes.
