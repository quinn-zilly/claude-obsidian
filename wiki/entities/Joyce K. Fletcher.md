# Joyce K. Fletcher

Nav: [[index]] | [[Gender & Careers MOC]] | [[Leadership MOC]]

Professor at the **Simmons School of Management** and affiliate of Simmons' Center for Gender in Organizations. Known for feminist analyses of **gender, power, and relational practice at work** — most notably the book *Disappearing Acts: Gender, Power and Relational Practice at Work* (MIT Press, 1999) and her earlier "Castrating the Female Advantage" (1994).

## In the wiki
- Author, [[fletcher-2004-paradox-postheroic-leadership|Fletcher (2004): The Paradox of Postheroic Leadership]] — coined the critical framing of [[Postheroic Leadership]] and the [[Doing Gender, Doing Power, Doing Leadership|"doing gender / doing power / doing leadership"]] mechanism.
