# Landy & Farr

Frank J. Landy and James L. Farr, I/O psychologists whose 1980 *Psychological Bulletin* review, "Performance Rating," is the field's landmark statement on rating-scale formats.

Key contribution relevant to the wiki:
- **Landy & Farr (1980)** reviewed decades of rating-format research and concluded that **scale format has only modest effects on rating quality** — effectively calling a *moratorium* on format research and redirecting the field toward the **cognitive and social process** of rating ([[denisi-murphy-2017-100-years-appraisal|DeNisi & Murphy, 2017]]; [[pulakos-2019-evolution-of-pm|Pulakos et al., 2019]]).
