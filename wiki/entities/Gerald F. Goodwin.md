# Gerald F. Goodwin

Team researcher associated with the **U.S. Army Research Institute for the Behavioral and Social Sciences**; co-editor of *Team Effectiveness in Complex Organizations* (2009) and Salas collaborator on team-performance frameworks.

## Key Contributions (cited in wiki)
- [[salas-et-al-2009-wisdom-of-collectives|Salas, Rosen, Burke & Goodwin (2009)]] — update of teamwork competencies.
- Salas, Stagl, Burke & Goodwin (2007) — integrative theoretical framework of team performance (review identifying 138 team models).
- Mathieu, Heffner, Goodwin, Salas & Cannon-Bowers (2000) — influence of shared mental models on team process/performance.
