# Angelo DeNisi

I/O psychologist and management scholar (A.B. Freeman School of Business, Tulane University). Major figure in **performance appraisal and performance management** research, especially the **cognitive approach** to appraisal (how raters acquire, organize, recall, and integrate performance information) and **feedback intervention** theory.

Key contributions relevant to the wiki:
- Cognitive model of the appraisal process (DeNisi, Cafferty & Meglino 1984); *A Cognitive Approach to Performance Appraisal* (2006).
- With Kluger, the landmark **Feedback Intervention Theory** meta-analysis (Kluger & DeNisi 1996) — feedback does not reliably improve performance and can harm it.
- DeNisi & Smith (2014) model of PM and firm-level performance; co-author of the 100-year centennial review [[denisi-murphy-2017-100-years-appraisal|DeNisi & Murphy (2017)]].
