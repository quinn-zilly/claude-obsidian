# Joseph G. Ponterotto

Counseling psychologist at **Fordham University** (Psychological and Educational Services, Lincoln Center). Works on multicultural counseling, qualitative research methods, and philosophy of science in counseling psychology.

In [[Ponterotto-2005-Qualitative-Research-Paradigms|Ponterotto (2005)]] he provides a widely-cited primer mapping four [[Research Paradigm|research paradigms]] across five [[Philosophy of Science Parameters|philosophy-of-science parameters]], and argues counseling psychology is undergoing a paradigm shift toward methodological pluralism. He earlier framed qualitative methods as the "fifth force in psychology" (Ponterotto 2002) and surveyed qualitative-research training across counseling psychology programs (Ponterotto 2005, *Teaching of Psychology*).
