# Robert T. Thibault

Meta-researcher at the Meta-Research Innovation Center at Stanford (METRICS), Stanford University. Lead author of [[thibault-2023-open-science-2-0|Open Science 2.0 (2023)]]. Works on preregistration, reproducibility, and registration–publication discrepancies in clinical and psychological research.
