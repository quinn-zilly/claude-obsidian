# Wesley Cohen

## Role

Economist of innovation and R&D. With [[Daniel Levinthal]], originated the **[[Absorptive Capacity]]** construct.

## Key Contributions

- **Cohen & Levinthal (1989)** — "Innovation and Learning: The Two Faces of R&D" (*Economic Journal*).
- **Cohen & Levinthal (1990)** — "Absorptive Capacity: A New Perspective on Learning and Innovation" (*Administrative Science Quarterly*) — the seminal, most-cited statement of the construct.
- **Cohen & Levinthal (1994)** — "Fortune Favors the Prepared Firm" (*Management Science*).

- Referenced throughout [[roberts-2012-absorptive-capacity-is-review|Roberts et al. (2012)]] as the construct's origin.
