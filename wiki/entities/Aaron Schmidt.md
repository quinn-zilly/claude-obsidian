# Aaron Schmidt

Nav: [[index]] | [[Multiple Goal Self-Regulation]]

IO psychologist. University of Minnesota (at time of chapter). Work on multiple-goal self-regulation and self-efficacy debates. Co-developer of empirical paradigm for studying within-person goal allocation dynamics.

## Key Contributions

- **Schmidt & DeShon (2007, 2009, 2010):** multi-goal allocation studies; SE × ambiguity + challenge moderators
- **Schmidt & Dolis (2009):** dual-goal expectancy moderates allocation strategy
- **Schmidt, Dolis & Tolli (2009):** replication under environmental volatility
- **Vancouver, Weinhardt & Schmidt (2010):** computational model of dynamic multi-goal pursuit
