# Julia E. Hoch

Nav: [[index]] | [[Teams & Org Behavior MOC]]

Management scholar (California State University–Northridge). Works on leadership forms, shared/virtual team leadership, and construct validity in leadership research.

## Key Contributions

- **[[hoch-et-al-2018-ethical-authentic-servant-vs-transformational|Hoch, Bommer, Dulebohn & Wu (2018)]]** — meta-analytic incremental-validity test of ethical, authentic, and servant leadership vs. transformational leadership.
- Hoch & Kozlowski (2014) on shared leadership in virtual teams (cited in [[Shared Leadership]]).
