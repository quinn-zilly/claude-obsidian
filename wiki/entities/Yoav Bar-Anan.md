# Yoav Bar-Anan

Israeli social psychologist. Associate Professor at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, Beer Sheva, Israel (at time of Utopia I publication). Co-author with [[Brian Nosek]] on the Scientific Utopia series.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[nosek-bar-anan-2012-scientific-utopia-i|Scientific Utopia I: Opening Scientific Communication]] (2012) with Nosek — one of the foundational reform papers in the [[Open Science]] movement
- Research focus: implicit attitudes, attitude measurement, prejudice, evaluative conditioning
