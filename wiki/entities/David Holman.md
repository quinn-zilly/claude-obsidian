# David Holman

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work Design (Concept)]]

Work and organizational psychologist at the Alliance Manchester Business School, University of Manchester. Co-author of [[parker-vandenbroeck-holman-2017-work-design-influences|Parker, Van den Broeck & Holman (2017)]].

His cross-national, comparative work is central to the institutional strand of the work-design-influences literature — studying how national [[Institutional Regimes & Employment Regime Theory|institutional regimes]] (e.g., the "Nordic advantage" of social democratic regimes) shape job discretion and cognitive demand across countries (Holman et al., 2009; Holman, 2013), and how international supply chains and client-organization choices affect supplier work designs.
