# James Breaugh

I-O psychologist (University of Missouri–St. Louis); a leading authority on **[[Employee Recruitment|employee recruitment]]** and **[[Realistic Job Preview|realistic job previews]]**. Author of the widely-cited definitions of recruitment (Breaugh 1992, 2008) and of the first *Annual Review of Psychology* review on the topic, [[breaugh-2013-employee-recruitment|Breaugh (2013)]].
