# Paul DiMaggio

American sociologist and organizational theorist. Professor at Princeton University (later NYU). Co-author with [[Walter Powell]] of the foundational 1983 paper on [[Institutional Isomorphism]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the theory of institutional isomorphism and the concept of the [[Organizational Field]] (DiMaggio & Powell 1983)
- Identified the three mechanisms of isomorphic change: coercive, mimetic, and normative
- Earlier work on cultural entrepreneurship and the emergence of dominant organizational forms in the arts (DiMaggio 1981, 1982)
- Research on field structuration as the process by which organizational fields become institutionally defined

## Institutional Affiliation

Yale University (at time of 1983 paper) → Princeton University → New York University
