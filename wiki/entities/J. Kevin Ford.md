# J. Kevin Ford

J. Kevin Ford is an industrial-organizational psychologist and leading scholar of workplace training, transfer of training, and organizational learning. He is co-author (with Kurt Kraiger) of a major 2021 review of the science of workplace instruction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of [[kraiger-ford-2021-workplace-instruction]]
- Foundational work on training transfer, transfer climate, and the Baldwin & Ford (1988) transfer model
- Research on learning organizations and continuous improvement

## Affiliation

Professor of Psychology, Michigan State University.
