# Raymond A. Noe

I-O psychologist at Ohio State University. Specializes in training and development, motivation to learn, and employee development.

## Key Works

- Noe (1986) — early model of how trainee characteristics (motivation, self-efficacy) influence learning
- Noe & Wilk (1993) — social support and work conditions → participation in developmental activities
- Colquitt, LePine & Noe (2000) — integrative theory of training motivation (meta-analytic path analysis)
- Noe, Clarke & Klein (2014) — learning in organizations: locating it, measuring it, improving it
- **Bell et al. (2017)** — co-author, centennial JAP review → [[bell-et-al-2017-100-years-training-development]]
