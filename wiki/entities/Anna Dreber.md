# Anna Dreber

Economist at Stockholm School of Economics. Leading researcher on prediction markets as a tool for assessing scientific replicability. Co-author on multiple replication prediction projects (Dreber et al. 2015; Camerer et al. 2016, 2018; Forsell et al. 2019; Ebersole et al. 2020).

Key contribution: showed that market prices for replication contracts correlate r=0.52 with actual replication outcomes — making replicability partially predictable *before* costly replication. 72% accuracy on dichotomous prediction.

Co-author on [[nosek-2022-replicability-robustness-reproducibility]] — provided prediction market data for Figure 2.
