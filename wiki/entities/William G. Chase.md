# William G. Chase

Cognitive psychologist (1940–1983). With [[Herbert A. Simon]], authored the classic Chase & Simon (1973) studies of chess memory establishing [[Chunking|chunk-based]] pattern retrieval as the mechanism of chess expertise. With [[K. Anders Ericsson]], developed [[Skilled Memory Theory]] (Chase & Ericsson 1981, 1982) from the digit-span training studies (subject SF: 82 digits).
