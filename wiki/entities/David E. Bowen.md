# David E. Bowen

Management scholar (G. Robert & Katherine Herberger Chair in Global Management, Thunderbird School of Global Management). Works at the intersection of **HRM, service management, and [[Organizational Justice|justice]]** — notably the idea that just treatment of employees "spills over" to customer satisfaction.

## Contributions

- Co-author, [[cropanzano-bowen-gilliland-2007-management-organizational-justice|Cropanzano, Bowen & Gilliland (2007)]] — managing justice across five HR systems.
- Bowen, Gilliland & Folger (1999) — justice-driven employee OCBs spilling over to customers.
