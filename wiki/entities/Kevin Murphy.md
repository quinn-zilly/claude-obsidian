# Kevin Murphy

I/O psychologist (University of Limerick; formerly Penn State, Colorado State). Leading authority on **performance appraisal**, psychometrics, and the **social/contextual** view of appraisal.

Key contributions relevant to the wiki:
- With Jeanette Cleveland, *Understanding Performance Appraisal: Social, Organizational and Goal-Oriented Perspectives* (1995) — reframed appraisal as a **motivated act in a social context**, not a pure measurement/judgment task.
- Murphy & Balzer (1986): traditional [[Rater Error Measures]] are uncorrelated with rating accuracy — a result that ended the field's reliance on error measures.
- Murphy et al. (2004): raters pursuing different goals give different ratings.
- Co-author of the centennial review [[denisi-murphy-2017-100-years-appraisal|DeNisi & Murphy (2017)]].
