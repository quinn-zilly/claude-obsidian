# Andrea Hollingshead

**Andrea B. Hollingshead** is a communication scholar and Professor of Communication, Psychology, and Management & Organizations at the University of Southern California (USC Annenberg). Her research examines how people communicate expertise and share knowledge in groups across offline, online, and hybrid settings.

**TMS contribution:** a leading figure on the communication and retrieval side of transactive memory. Her work shows that a [[Transactive Memory Systems|TMS]] forms through communication about expertise and that perceptions of coworkers' relative expertise shape individual learning and information allocation. With David Brandon she developed the "matching tasks, expertise, and people" framing of organizational TMS.

In [[ren-argote-2011-tms|Ren & Argote (2011)]] she is cited for the communication & retrieval perspective; her 1998 questionnaires are listed among established TMS measures for dyads.
