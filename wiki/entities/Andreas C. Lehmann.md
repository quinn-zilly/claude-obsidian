# Andreas C. Lehmann

Music psychologist specializing in the acquisition of expert musical skill (sight-reading, accompaniment). Co-author with [[K. Anders Ericsson]] of [[ericsson-lehmann-1996-expert-exceptional-performance|Ericsson & Lehmann (1996)]] and of studies showing that expert pianists' sight-reading differences are predicted by *specific training opportunities*, not general talent.
