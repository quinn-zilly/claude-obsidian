# Ellen Ernst Kossek

Distinguished management professor (Krannert School of Management, Purdue University) and a leading scholar of **work–family/work–life, flexibility, and gender in organizations**. Her prolific body of work spans boundary-management styles (Kossek & Lautsch), family-supportive supervisor behaviors (the Hammer et al. 2011 RCT), workplace social support meta-analyses (Kossek, Pichler, Bodner & Hammer 2011), and diversity climate (Kossek & Zonia 1993 — a seed for inclusion research).
