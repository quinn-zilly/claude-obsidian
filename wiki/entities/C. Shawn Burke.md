# C. Shawn Burke

Team researcher at the Institute for Simulation & Training (UCF); frequent Salas collaborator. Notable for work on **team adaptation**, **shared leadership**, and teamwork models.

## Key Contributions (cited in wiki)
- Salas, Sims & Burke (2005) — the [[Big Five of Teamwork]] model.
- **Burke, Stagl, Salas, Pierce & Kendall (2006)** — [[burke-et-al-2006-team-adaptation]] — the classic [[Team Adaptation (Burke Cross-Level Model)|four-phase adaptive cycle]] (situation assessment → plan formulation → plan execution → team learning); seminal predecessor to the [[Team Adaptation (Stimulus-Based Model)|Pearsall et al. (2025) model]].
- Burke, Fiore & Salas (2004) — shared cognition enabling shared leadership and adaptability.
- [[salas-et-al-2009-wisdom-of-collectives|Salas, Rosen, Burke & Goodwin (2009)]] — update of teamwork competencies.
