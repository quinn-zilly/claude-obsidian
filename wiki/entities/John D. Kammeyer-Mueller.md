# John D. Kammeyer-Mueller

Organizational psychologist. Department of Management, University of Florida (as of 2012).

Research focus: turnover, job attitudes, organizational socialization, temporal dynamics of work attitudes.

## Key Contributions

- **Turnover and job attitudes** — Kammeyer-Mueller et al. (2005): temporal shifts in turnover processes; satisfaction with work itself most strongly predicts withdrawal cognitions and turnover intentions; attitudes toward job and org. have independent effects on turnover
- **[[Situational Antecedents of Job Attitudes]]** — co-author of comprehensive job attitudes review
