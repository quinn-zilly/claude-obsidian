# Christopher G. Myers

Management/organizational-behavior scholar at Johns Hopkins University. Studies individual and vicarious learning at work, knowledge sharing, and learning in health care. Author of [[myers-2018-coactive-vicarious-learning|Myers (2018)]], the *AMR* theory paper introducing [[Coactive Vicarious Learning (CVL)]]; also known for work with Scott DeRue on vicarious learning (Myers & DeRue, 2017).
