# Michael Frese

Nav: [[index]]

I/O psychologist. Department of Management & Organization, National University of Singapore and Leuphana University of Lueneburg. Pioneer of Action Regulation Theory and personal initiative research.

## Key Contributions

- Action regulation theory (Frese & Zapf 1994) — plans as bridge between goal choice and striving; hierarchical action structure
- Personal initiative (Frese & Fay 2001) — self-starting, proactive, persistent work behavior concept
- Error management training and self-regulation (Keith & Frese 2005)
- Entrepreneurship research — personality traits, action planning, and business success in Africa (Frese, Krauss et al. 2007); meta-analysis of personality → entrepreneurship (Rauch & Frese 2007)
- Psychology of planning in organizations (Frese, Mumford & Gibson 2015)
- Dynamic performance and motivation over time (Sonnentag & Frese 2012)

## Major Works

- Frese & Zapf (1994) — Action regulation theory
- Frese & Fay (2001) — Personal initiative
