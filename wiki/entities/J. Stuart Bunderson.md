# J. Stuart Bunderson

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work as a Calling MOC]]

Organizational scholar (Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis). With Jeffery Thompson, developed the neoclassical and later the integrative [[Transcendent Calling|transcendent]] conceptions of calling. Also known for research on teams, expertise, and status.

## Key contributions

- **Bunderson & Thompson (2009)** — zookeeper study; neoclassical calling; the calling double-edged sword.
- **[[Thompson-Bunderson-2019-Work-as-a-Calling|Thompson & Bunderson (2019)]]** — *Annual Review* calling taxonomy and the inner × outer requiredness framework.
