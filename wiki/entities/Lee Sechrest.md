# Lee Sechrest

Psychologist and methodologist, University of Arizona (Evaluation Group for Analysis of Data). Co-author of [[Sechrest-Figueredo-1993-Program-Evaluation|Sechrest & Figueredo (1993)]], the *Annual Review of Psychology* chapter arguing for an ecological view of program evaluation and a return to strong theory + multivariate methods.

Long-standing advocate for quantifying [[Strength and Integrity of Treatment]] (Sechrest et al. 1979) and for multiple, complementary sources of knowledge in evaluation. Frequent collaborator: [[Aurelio José Figueredo]].
