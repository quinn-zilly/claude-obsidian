# Mikhail A. Wolfson

Management scholar at the University of Kentucky (Gatton College of Business and Economics), working on informal learning, human capital resources, and team/organizational effectiveness.

## Key Work

- [[tannenbaum-wolfson-2022-informal-field-based-learning]]: *Annual Review of Organizational Psychology and Organizational Behavior* 9 — IFBL review and CAM-OS framework
- Wolfson, Tannenbaum, Mathieu & Maynard (2018): "A cross-level investigation of informal field-based learning and performance improvements" — *J. Appl. Psychol.* 103 (origin of the IFBL construct/definition)
- Wolfson, Mathieu, Tannenbaum & Maynard (2019): "Informal field-based learning and work design" — *J. Appl. Psychol.* 104
- Wolfson & Mathieu (2021): deploying human capital resources — *Academy of Management Journal*

## Contributions

Co-originator of the [[Informal Field-Based Learning (IFBL)]] construct and its three-behavior taxonomy (feedback/reflection, vicarious, experimentation). Frequent collaborator with [[Scott I. Tannenbaum]] and [[John E. Mathieu]].
