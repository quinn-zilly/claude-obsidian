# Gary Johns

Nav: [[index]] | [[Work Design (Concept)]]

Organizational behavior scholar at Concordia University (John Molson School of Business). Co-author of [[parker-morgeson-johns-2017-100-years-work-design|Parker, Morgeson & Johns (2017)]].

Known for foundational work on **the essential impact of context** in organizational behavior (Johns, 2006), curvilinear job design effects — "can job scope be too high?" (Xie & Johns, 1995, finding a U-shaped job-complexity/exhaustion relationship) — mediating/moderating effects in job design (Johns, Xie & Fang, 1992), personality × job scope interactions (Raja & Johns, 2010), and unintended consequences of job design (Johns, 2010).
