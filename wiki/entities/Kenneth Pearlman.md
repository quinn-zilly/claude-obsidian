# Kenneth Pearlman

I-O psychologist specializing in job/work analysis and occupational information. Co-author of [[Shippmann-et-al-2000-practice-of-competency-modeling|Shippmann et al. (2000)]] and a member of **APDOT**, where he argued the term "skills" had become a "Tower of Babel" and pushed for the Content Model adopted in [[Occupational Information Network (O-NET)|O*NET]]. His work with Sanchez (Pearlman & Sanchez, 2010) frames job analysis as "seldom an end in itself" — a characterization [[Sanchez-Levine-2012-rise-fall-job-analysis|Sanchez & Levine (2012)]] argue unintentionally lowered the field's status.
