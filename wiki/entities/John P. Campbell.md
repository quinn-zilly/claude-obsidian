# John P. Campbell

I/O psychologist at the **University of Minnesota**. A foundational figure in the modeling of individual job performance; led theoretical work emerging from **Project A** (Army Selection and Classification Project) and authored the **Campbell (2012) eight-factor** revision that anchors the [[Campbell Model of Job Performance|near-consensus latent structure]]. Co-author of [[campbell-wiernik-2015-modeling-assessment-work-performance|Campbell & Wiernik (2015)]].
