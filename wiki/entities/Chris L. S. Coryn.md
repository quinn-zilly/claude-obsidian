# Chris L. S. Coryn

Evaluation researcher at The Evaluation Center, Western Michigan University. Lead author of the 2011 systematic review of [[Theory-Driven Evaluation]] practice. Has raised the critique that TDE writers describe *what* to do but not *how* to do it.

- **[[coryn-et-al-2011-theory-driven-evaluation-review]]** (with Noakes, Westine & Schröter, 2011) — *American Journal of Evaluation*.
