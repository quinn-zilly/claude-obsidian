# Astrid C. Homan

Nav: [[index]] | [[Teams & Org Behavior MOC]]

Professor of Work and Organizational Psychology, University of Amsterdam. Research centers on **team diversity**, diversity beliefs, faultlines, information elaboration, and the leadership of diverse teams. A frequent collaborator in the [[Daan van Knippenberg|van Knippenberg]] diversity tradition and coauthor of the [[Categorization-Elaboration Model]].

- Lead author, [[homan-et-al-2020-leading-diversity-functional|Homan et al. (2020): Leading Diversity]] — the [[Leading Diversity (LeaD) Model|LeaD model]] of functional diversity leadership.
