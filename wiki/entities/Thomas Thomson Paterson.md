# Thomas Thomson Paterson

Scottish professor who developed the **[[Paterson Job Evaluation|Paterson method]]** of job evaluation (concepts and rules published ~1963), grading jobs by the kind and complexity of **decisions** required. The six decision bands (A–F) remain the basis of one of the most widely used job-evaluation systems in Africa. His decision-based logic is closely related to Elliott Jaques' [[Stratified Systems Theory]].
