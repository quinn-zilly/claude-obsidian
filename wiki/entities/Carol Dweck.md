# Carol Dweck

Psychologist (Stanford), originator of the **implicit theories / mindset** research program and the concept of [[Growth Mindset]] (*Mindset: The New Psychology of Success*, 2006). Her work on the malleability of intelligence underpins several education [[Wise Interventions]].

Relevant to the vault via [[Walton-2014-Wise-Psychological-Interventions|Walton (2014)]]: co-author of the voter-turnout study (Bryan, Walton, Rogers & Dweck 2011); the Blackwell, Trzesniewski & Dweck (2007) growth-mindset intervention; and Mueller & Dweck (1998) showing intelligence praise can *undermine* motivation.
