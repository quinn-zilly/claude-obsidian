# Christopher P. Cerasoli

I-O psychologist at The Group for Organizational Effectiveness (gOE). Lead author of the first meta-analysis of [[Informal Learning Behaviors (ILBs)]] ([[cerasoli-et-al-2018-informal-learning-behaviors-meta-analysis|Cerasoli et al., 2018]]). Also known for meta-analytic work on intrinsic motivation and performance (Cerasoli, Nicklin & Ford, 2014).
