# Winfred Arthur Jr.

Industrial-organizational psychologist at **Texas A&M University** (Department of Psychology).

## Key Work

- [[arthur-et-al-2003-training-meta-analysis]] — meta-analysis of training design/evaluation features and effectiveness
- Lead developer of the SAMD (sample-adjusted meta-analytic deviancy) outlier detection statistic (Huffcutt & Arthur, 1995)
- Co-author of meta-analysis methodology reference: Arthur, Bennett & Huffcutt (2001)

## Collaborators

- Winston Bennett Jr. (Air Force Research Laboratory)
