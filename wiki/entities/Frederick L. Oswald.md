# Frederick L. Oswald

I/O psychologist and psychometrician, Department of Psychology, **Rice University** (Houston). Works on personality structure, selection, big-data methods, and validity.

Central contributions relevant to this vault:

- Co-author (with [[Leaetta M. Hough]]) of the "beyond the Big Five" agenda and the review of the four taxonomic approaches to personality structure (Oswald & Hough 2011; Hough, Oswald & Ock 2015).
- Advocate of **[[Nomological-Web Clustering]]** and of using generalizability theory + synthetic validity to refine personality taxonomies.
