# William Revelle

Personality/quantitative psychologist (Northwestern University), author of the R **`psych`** package — the standard toolkit for reliability, factor analysis, and omega estimation. Longtime advocate for model-based reliability ($\omega_h$, $\omega_t$) over coefficient alpha (Revelle & Zinbarg 2009; Zinbarg et al. 2005).
