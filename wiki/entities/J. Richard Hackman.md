# J. Richard Hackman

Nav: [[index]] | [[Job Characteristics Model]]

Social and organizational psychologist (Harvard University; 1940–2013). With Greg Oldham, co-originator of **Job Characteristics Theory** (Hackman & Oldham 1976, 1980) and the **Job Diagnostic Survey**. Earlier work with Lawler (1971) on the four core characteristics. Later a foundational theorist of **team effectiveness** (Hackman 1987 normative model of group effectiveness).

## Key Contributions

- **Hackman & Lawler (1971):** four job characteristics → internal work motivation.
- **Job Characteristics Theory** and the **JDS** (with Oldham).
- **Hackman (1987):** model of team effectiveness (effort × knowledge/skill × performance strategies).
