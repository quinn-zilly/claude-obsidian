# Steven Kerr

Organizational behavior scholar. Ph.D., City University of New York. Faculty at Ohio State University, then University of Southern California (Dean of Faculty 1985–89). President of the Academy of Management 1989–90. Later: VP of Corporate Management Development at General Electric (managing Crotonville); visiting professor at University of Michigan.

> [!note] Not to be confused with [[Norbert Kerr]] (HARKing, social psychology, Michigan State)

## Key Contribution

[[kerr-1995-folly-rewarding-A|Kerr (1975/1995)]] — "On the Folly of Rewarding A, While Hoping for B." Argued that formal reward systems routinely reinforce the opposite of stated organizational goals across politics, war, medicine, universities, business, sports, and government. Now part of management lexicon; widely reprinted Academy Classic.
