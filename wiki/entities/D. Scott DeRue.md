# D. Scott DeRue

Nav: [[index]] | [[Teams & Org Behavior MOC]]

Management scholar (Stephen M. Ross School of Business, University of Michigan, at the time of the 2011 paper; later Dean of Ross). Works on leadership, leader development, and team processes.

## Key Contributions

- **[[derue-et-al-2011-trait-behavioral-leadership-integration|DeRue, Nahrgang, Wellman & Humphrey (2011)]]** — integrative trait-behavioral meta-analysis of leadership effectiveness; the anchor for construct-redundancy testing in leadership research.
