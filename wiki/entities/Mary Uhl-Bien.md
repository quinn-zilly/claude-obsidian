# Mary Uhl-Bien

Nav: [[index]] | [[Leadership MOC]]

Management scholar known for relational leadership, complexity leadership, and followership theory. Lead author of the agenda-setting followership review.

## Key Contributions

- **[[uhl-bien-et-al-2014-followership-theory-review|Uhl-Bien, Riggio, Lowe & Carsten (2014)]]** — defined [[Followership Theory]] and its role-based vs. constructionist frameworks.
- Relational leadership theory (Uhl-Bien 2006) and the discursive/relational approach (Fairhurst & Uhl-Bien 2012), both cited across the [[Followership Theory]] and [[Shared Leadership]] literatures.
