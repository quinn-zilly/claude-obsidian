# Shaker Zahra

## Role

Professor of Strategy and Entrepreneurship at the **Carlson School of Management, University of Minnesota**. Known for work at the intersection of entrepreneurship, dynamic capabilities, and organizational knowledge.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of the landmark review on knowledge integration (Zahra, Neubaum & Hayton, 2020)
- Prior work on absorptive capacity (Zahra & George, 2002) — reconceptualizing Cohen & Levinthal's absorptive capacity as a dynamic capability with potential and realized components
- Work on knowledge integration within and across organizational boundaries
- Research on dynamic capabilities and the knowledge-based view
