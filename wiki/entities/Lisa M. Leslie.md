# Lisa M. Leslie

Professor of Management and Organizations at the **Stern School of Business, New York University**. Researches diversity, diversity ideologies and climate, gender, and workplace inequality.

Lead author of [[leslie-et-al-2020-diversity-ideologies-meta-analysis|Leslie, Bono, Kim & Beaver (2020)]], the *JAP* meta-analysis distinguishing colorblindness, meritocracy, assimilation, and multiculturalism as distinct [[Diversity Ideologies|diversity ideologies]] with divergent effects on intergroup relations.
