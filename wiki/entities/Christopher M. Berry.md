# Christopher M. Berry

Nav: [[index]] | [[entities/_index]]

Industrial-organizational psychologist, Kelley School of Business, Indiana University. Co-author of [[Sackett-et-al-2022-validity-overcorrection|Sackett et al. (2022)]]. His earlier Berry et al. (2007) work established that a single range-restriction correction factor should not be applied uniformly across studies with different restriction mechanisms — a conceptual seed of the 2022 paper.
