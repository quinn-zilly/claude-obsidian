# Leif D. Nelson

Behavioral scientist at the Haas School of Business, University of California, Berkeley. Co-author of [[simmons-et-al-2011-false-positive-psychology|False-Positive Psychology (2011)]] and co-founder of the Data Colada blog with [[Joseph P. Simmons]] and [[Uri Simonsohn]].

## Key contributions

- Co-coined [[Researcher Degrees of Freedom]].
- Co-developer of p-curve analysis for detecting [[P-hacking]].
