Last ingest: 2026-07-12 — **wise interventions + program evaluation** (2 sources), extending the **[[Learning & Development MOC]]** (Walton) and the evaluation concept cluster (S&F). Spine: **interventions work when a precise, well-founded theory meets the real recursive/multi-determined context — not when the intervention is merely big or the method merely rigorous.** **(1) [[Walton-2014-Wise-Psychological-Interventions|Walton (2014)]]** (*Current Directions in Psychological Science* 23(1):73–82) — names **[[Wise Interventions]]**: ordinary, brief, precise interventions that alter one psychological process in the normal course of life. Three properties: **precise** (well-founded theory in a brief exercise; inaccurate theory misfires — the [[Growth Mindset]] "you're so smart" trap), **target [[Recursive Processes (Wise Interventions)|recursive processes]]** ("movie not snapshot"; largest effects most distal; real-world factors act as proximal outcomes carrying effects forward), **context-dependent — not silver bullets** (process must matter; exercise must land; context must afford the loop). Direct (reading/writing) > indirect (new-experience) interventions — Schulz nursing-home reversal shows indirect fragility. Exemplars: [[Social Belonging Intervention]] (Walton & Cohen 2011, halved Black–White grade gap over 3 yrs), [[Values Affirmation]] (Cohen 2009), [[Saying-Is-Believing]] (recipients author the message). Term traces via [[Claude Steele]]'s "wise schooling"/[[Stereotype Threat]]. **(2) [[Sechrest-Figueredo-1993-Program-Evaluation|Sechrest & Figueredo (1993)]]** (*Annual Review of Psychology* 44:645–674; ingested via OCR — the `.raw` `.md` was an empty stub, real text in `.raw/Input/…pdf`) — an **[[Ecological Paradigm in Evaluation|ecological]]** view: **Newtonian vs Darwinian** paradigms (mainstream social science's single-variable experiment has poor external validity; the I₁→D₁→O₁→U₁ path model fails in a multi-determined, multi-stakeholder "Darwinian jungle"), a postindustrial social revolution repositioning the evaluator as an embedded stakeholder, **[[Evaluation Is Not a Field]]** (an enterprise for deciding worth; progress = concepts + methods, not findings), a skeptical read of the qualitative "movement" (discovery vs justification; PUSH/Excel case = values not facts), the **[[Five Fundamental Issues of Evaluation]]** (Shadish et al.: programming→[[Theory-Driven Evaluation]], knowledge construction, valuing, use, practice), **[[Formative vs Summative Evaluation]]** drift (formative easier & more comforting; Rossi's ≈0 expected impact), **[[Prospective Evaluation]]** (GAO/PEMD forecasting), **[[Strength and Integrity of Treatment]]** (quantify dose+fidelity; Type-II guard), and a case for **multivariate methods** (common-factor modeling over stepwise; sequential canonical analysis; "rule causes *in*, not out"). **Cross-source spine:** both are about *intervention* at different grains (Walton = micro/psychological, S&F = macro/program) and both privilege **strong theory + external validity in real context**. **New: 12 concepts + 10 entities + 2 sources.** Updated [[Stereotype Threat]], [[Theory-Driven Evaluation]], [[Learning & Development MOC]], [[index]]. **Addresses c-001237–c-001260.** Candidate next: Yeager & Walton (2011) "not magic"; Cohen et al. (1) **[[revelle-condon-2019-reliability-alpha-to-omega|Revelle & Condon (2019)]]** (*Psychological Assessment*) — the α-to-ω tutorial. **[[Reliability]]** = a **variance ratio** (signal/total) and a *joint* property of test AND sample, not the test alone; reliable variance mixes **Trait+State+specific**, so **there is no single reliability** — design picks what you isolate (internal consistency vs dependability [short retest] vs stability [long retest] vs inter-rater). Ratings → **ICC1–3**; categorical → **Cohen's κ**. R `psych` (`omega`, `alpha`, `splitHalf`, `testRetest`, `ICC`, `cohen.kappa`). (2) **[[davison-davenport-2002-criterion-related-patterns|Davison & Davenport (2002)]]** (*Psychological Methods*) — the **method-origin primary source** for **[[Criterion Profile Analysis|CPA]]** (vault previously footed CPA only on the [[wiernik-et-al-2021-meta-analytic-cpa|Wiernik 2021 MACPA]] extension).
