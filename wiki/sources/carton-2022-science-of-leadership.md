# Carton (2022): The Science of Leadership — A Theoretical Model and Research Agenda

> [!abstract] TL;DR
> A meta-theoretical map of the post-2010 leadership literature built on two crossed distinctions: **[[Leaders vs Leading]]** (person vs process) and **[[Leadership as Cause vs Consequence]]** (independent vs dependent variable). Carton's sharpest argument: the dominant **[[Lumped vs Split Conceptualizations of Leading|lumped]]** approach to *leading* should give way to **split** approaches that isolate single behaviors.

## The organizing framework (2×2)

Two features locate any leadership study:

- **Leaders vs leading** — the *leader* is a person with status/authority (a higher, temporally stable level of analysis); *leading* is a social-influence process (a lower level; behavior varies within a leader day to day, sometimes more than between leaders).
- **Cause vs consequence** — is leadership the independent variable (predicting outcomes) or the dependent variable (something to be explained)?

Crossing them yields four categories:

| | Dependent variable (consequence) | Independent variable (cause) |
|---|---|---|
| **Leader** | Leader as an *attribute* (who becomes/counts as a leader — categorization, [[Implicit Leadership Theories|emergence, evaluation]]) | Leader as IV (leader-level attributes → outcomes) |
| **Leading** | Leading as DV — *directing attention* / attributions about the process | Leading as IV (leader *behaviors* → outcomes) |

> [!info] Where the field crowds
> Most work sits in **leading-as-independent-variable**, and within it, in **lumped** frameworks. Leading-as-dependent-variable is comparatively neglected.

## Lumped vs split conceptualizations

See [[Lumped vs Split Conceptualizations of Leading]]. **Lumpers** bundle many behaviors into a single meta-construct (the classic case: [[Transformational and Charismatic Leadership|transformational leadership]]); **splitters** isolate single, observable behaviors. Carton acknowledges lumped constructs' practical convenience (esp. for leader development) but argues they obscure causal mechanisms and impede cumulative science — echoing [[Construct Redundancy (Leadership)|construct-redundancy]] concerns.

> [!key-insight] The research-agenda payoff
> Locating a study in the 2×2 tells you what it can and cannot establish, and reveals under-populated cells. The prescriptive core: **split leading into isolable behaviors** to build precise, additive causal evidence.
