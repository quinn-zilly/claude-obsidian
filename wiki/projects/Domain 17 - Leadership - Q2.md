# Question

Discuss how I-O psychology leadership research has changed over the last several decades. In your response, consider the following: (a) describe how the role of “leader” has evolved; (b) specify newer or emergent theories of leadership that you deem relevant or important, and (c) choose one theory that you believe can help leaders improve organizational performance in the 21st century, incorporating research support.

# Outline

## Role of Leader Evolution

1) Leadership Traits
2) Behavioral Styles
3) Contingent Leadership
4) Transformational, Leader-Member Exchange, Shared Leadership, Team Leadership, Gender

## Emergent Theories of Shared Leadership

**I-O psychology should focus on social relationships of leadership and leadership behaviors.**
- Relevance of Shared Leadership ([[zhu-et-al-2018-shared-leadership-review]])
- Effectiveness of shared leadership [[wang-et-al-2014-shared-leadership-team-effectiveness]]
- Leadership behaviors are stronger predictors of effectiveness than traits [[derue-et-al-2011-trait-behavioral-leadership-integration]]
- Leadership Emergence [[badura-et-al-2022-leadership-emergence-integrative-review]]
- Split leader from leading; leadership as cause vs. consequence [[carton-2022-science-of-leadership]]
- Formal vs informal team leadership functions [[morgeson-et-al-2010-leadership-in-teams-functional]]

**Adaptive Leadership Theory ([[derue-2011-adaptive-leadership-complex-adaptive-process]])**
